REGULATION\namending Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 concerning restrictive measures against Iran\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n\n\n(1)Council Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 of 23 March 2012 concerning restrictive measures against Iran gives effect to the measures provided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP.\n\n\n(2)On 31 July 2015, the Council adopted Decision (CFSP) 2015/1337 amending Decision 2010/413/CFSP and extending until 14 January 2016 the exemption provided for in paragraph 14 of Article 20 concerning acts and transactions carried out with regard to listed entities insofar as necessary for the execution of the obligations provided for in contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 or in ancillary contracts necessary for the execution of such obligations where the supply of Iranian crude oil and petroleum products or the proceeds derived from their supply are for the reimbursement of outstanding amounts with respect to contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 to persons or entities within the territories of Member States or under their jurisdiction, where those contracts specifically provide for such reimbursements.\n\n\n(3)The Council adopted Decision (CFSP) 2016/\u2026/ further extending the abovementioned exemption until 4 February 2016.\n\n\n(4)Further action by the Union is needed in order to implement this extension.\n\n\n(5)The High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and the European Commission propose to amend Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 accordingly.\n\n\n2016/0001 (NLE)\n\n\nJoint Proposal for a\n\n\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\n\n\namending Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 concerning restrictive measures against Iran\n\n\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\n\n\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof, \n\n\nHaving regard to Council Decision 2010/413/CFSP of 26 July 2010 concerning restrictive measures against Iran and repealing Common Position 2007/140/CFSP\u00a0\n1\n,\n\n\nHaving regard to the joint proposal of the High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and of the European Commission,\n\n\nWhereas:\n\n\n(1)Council Regulation (EU) No 267/2012\n2\n gives effect to the measures provided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP.\n\n\n(2)On 31 July 2015, the Council adopted Decision (CFSP) 2015/1337 amending Decision 2010/413/CFSP to extend until 14 January 2016 the exemption provided for in paragraph 14 of Article 20 concerning acts and transactions carried out with regard to listed entities insofar as necessary for the execution of the obligations provided for in contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 or in ancillary contracts necessary for the execution of such obligations where the supply of Iranian crude oil and petroleum products or the proceeds derived from their supply are for the reimbursement of outstanding amounts with respect to contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 to persons or entities within the territories of Member States or under their jurisdiction, where those contracts specifically provide for such reimbursements.\n\n\n(3)On \u2026 January 2016, the Council adopted Decision (CFSP) 2016/\u2026 further extending the above mentioned exemption until 4 February 2016.\n\n\n(4)This measure falls within the scope of the Treaty and regulatory action at the level of the Union is therefore necessary in order to implement it, in particular with a view to ensuring its uniform application by economic operators in all Member States.\n\n\n\n\n(5)Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 should therefore be amended accordingly,\n\n\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\n\n\nArticle 1\n\n\n\nIn point (b) of Article 28a of Regulation (EU) No 267/2012, the words \u2018until 14 January 2016\u2019 are replaced by the words \u2018until 4 February 2016\u2019.\n\n\nArticle 2 \n\n\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on the day of its publication.\n\n\nThis Regulation shall be binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\n\n\nDone at Brussels,\n\n\n\n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0For the Council\n\n\n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0The President\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n(1)\nOJ L 195, 27.7.2010.\u00a0p. 39.\n\n\n\n(2)\nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 of 23March 2012 concerning restrictive measures
