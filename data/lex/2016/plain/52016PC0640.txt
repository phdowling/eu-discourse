Following further technical consultations and correspondence, the Protocol was initialled by the Commission and Bosnia and Herzegovina on 18 July 2016.\n\n\nThe Commission proposes that the Council decides on the conclusion of the Protocol on behalf of the European Union and its Member States. Signature and provisional application of the Protocol are subject to a proposal for a separate Council decision. The conclusion of the Protocol on behalf of the European Atomic Energy Community (EAEC) follows a separate procedure, by which the Commission recommends that the Council gives its approval, pursuant to the second paragraph of Article 101 of the Treaty establishing the EAEC. \n\n\nThe attached proposal is for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Protocol.
