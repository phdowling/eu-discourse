The Agreement entered into force on 1 June 2004. \n\n\nAccording its Act of Accession, the Republic of Croatia undertakes to accede to the international agreements signed or concluded by the European Union and its Member States by means of a protocol to these agreements.\n\n\nThe attached proposal constitutes the legal instrument for conclusion of the Protocol to the Agreement in order to take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union.\n\n\nWith regards to rules of origin, the EU and the Arab Republic of Egypt agreed, by Decision No 1/2015 of the EU-Egypt Association Council of 21 September 2015\n1\n, that the new Protocol 4 to the Agreement will have to refer to the Regional Convention on pan-Euro-Mediterranean preferential rules of origin, which lays down the rules of origin and provides for cumulation of origin between the European Union, Egypt and other Contracting Parties as of 1 February 2016. Therefore, the article 3 (Rules of Origin) of the Protocol annexed to this decision will only cover the period between the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the EU and the entry into force of Decision No 1/2015.\n\n\nBy its Decision of 14 September 2012\n2\n, the Council authorised the Commission to open negotiations with the third countries concerned with a view to concluding the relevant protocols. The negotiations with the Arab Republic of Egypt were successfully concluded on 29 october 2015.\n\n\nThe Commission judges the results of the negotiations to be satisfactory and requests the Council to adopt the attached Decision on the conclusion of the Protocol after obtaining the consent of the European Parliament.\n\n\n2016/0121 (NLE)\n\n\nProposal for a\n\n\nCOUNCIL DECISION\n\n\non the conclusion, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, of a Protocol to the Euro-Mediterranean Agreement establishing an Association between the European Communities and their Member States, of the one part, and the Arab Republic of Egypt, of the other part, to take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union \n\n\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\n\n\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, and in particular Article\u00a0217, in conjunction with Article 218(6)(a) thereof,\n\n\nHaving regard to the Act of Accession of the Republic of Croatia, and in particular Article\u00a06 (2) thereof,\n\n\nHaving regard to the proposal from the European Commission,\n\n\nHaving regard to the consent of the European Parliament,\n\n\nWhereas:\n\n\n(1)The Euro-Mediterranean Agreement establishing an Association between the European Communities and their Member States, of the one part, and the Arab Republic of Egypt, of the other part\n3\n ('the Agreement'), was signed on 25 June 2001.
