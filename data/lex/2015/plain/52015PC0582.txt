"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEUROPEAN COMMISSION\nBrussels, 25.11.2015\nCOM(2015) 582 final\n2015/0265(NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the conclusion of the Cooperation Agreement on a Civil Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) between the European Community and its Member States of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part.\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n\n\n1.CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\n\n\nThe Republic of Korea is a country with capabilities in the space technology and applications. It is an active user of satellite navigation services in various sectors including transport, fleet management , science and geodesy.\n\n\nThe European Commission, on the basis of Council authorisation given on 18 July 2005, negotiated a Cooperation agreement on a Civil Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) between the European Community and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part( hereinafter referred to as 'the Agreement). The Agreement was then signed on 9 September 2006 pursuant to Council decision on the signing of 1 September 2006.\n\n\nThe signatory Member States of the European Union and the Republic of Korea have completed their procedures related to the entry into force of the Agreement.\n\n\nIn accordance with Article 218(6) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, the Commission proposes to the Council a Decision on the conclusion of the Agreement on behalf of the European Union after consent of the European Parliament.\n\n\nBulgaria, Croatia, and Romania are to become parties to the Agreement by the conclusion of a protocol in accordance with Art 6(2) of the Acts concerning the conditions of accession of the Republic of Bulgaria and Romania and of the Republic of Croatia respectively and the adjustments to the treaties on which the European Union is founded.\n\n\n2015/0265 (NLE)\n\n\nProposal for a\n\n\nCOUNCIL DECISION\n\n\non the conclusion of the Cooperation Agreement on a Civil Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) between the European Community and its Member States of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part.\n\n\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\n\n\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 172, in conjunction with Article\u00a0218(6)(a) thereof,\n\n\nHaving regard to the proposal from the European Commission,\n\n\nHaving regard to the consent of the European Parliament\n1\n,\n\n\nWhereas:\n\n\n(1)In accordance with Council Decision 2006/700/EC of 1 September 2006\n2\n, the Cooperation Agreement on a Civil Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) between the European Community and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part, was signed on 9 September 2006 subject to its conclusion at a later date.\n\n\n(2)The Agreement is to encourage, facilitate and enhance cooperation between the Parties in civil global satellite navigation.  \n\n\n(3)The agreement should be approved on behalf of the European Union.\n\n\n\n\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \n\n\nArticle 1\n\n\nThe Cooperation Agreement on a Civil Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) between the European Community and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part is hereby approved on behalf of the European Union. \n\n\nThe text of the Agreement is attached to this Decision.\n\n\nArticle 2\n\n\nThe President of the Council shall designate the person empowered, on behalf of the European Union to send the notification, provided for in Article 18(1) of the Agreement, in order to express the consent of the European Union to be bound by the Agreement and shall make the following notification:\n\n\n'As a consequence of the entry into force of the Treaty of Lisbon on 1 December 2009, the European Union has replaced and succeeded the European Community and from that date exercises all rights and assumes all obligations of the European Community. Therefore, references to \u201cthe European Community\u201d in the text of the Agreement are to be read as to \u201cthe European Union\u201d'.\n\n\nArticle 3\n\n\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption. It shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Union\n3\n.\n\n\nDone at Brussels,\n\n\n\n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0For the Council\n\n\n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0The President\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n(1)\nConsent given on [\u2026201.].\n\n\n\n(2)\nOJ L 288 of 19/10/2006, p. 30.\n\n\n\n(3)\nThe date of entry into force of the Agreement will be published in the Official Journal of the European Union by the General Secretariat of the Council.\n\n\n\n\n\nTop  \n  "
