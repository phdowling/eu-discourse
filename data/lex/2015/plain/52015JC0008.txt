action by the Union is needed in order to implement it.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 19 February 2015 Commission\r\nImplementing Regulation (EU) 2015/275 removed those THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof, \nHaving regard to the joint proposal of the\r\nHigh Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and of\r\nthe European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EC) No\r\n314/2004[3]\r\nimplements several measures provided for by Council Decision 2011/101/CFSP[4], including the freezing\r\nof funds and economic resources of certain natural or legal persons, entities\r\nand bodies. \n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Annex IV to Regulation\r\n(EC) No 314/2004 lists the persons and entities suspended from the freezing of\r\nfunds and economic resources under that Regulation. \n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 19 February 2015 the\r\nCouncil adopted Council Decision (CFSP) 2015/277[5]\r\namending Decision 2011/101/CFSP removing the names of five deceased persons\r\nfrom Annex I and II to that Decision.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 That measure\r\nfalls within the scope of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union\r\nand regulatory action at the level of the Union is therefore necessary in order\r\nto implement it, in particular with a view to ensuring its uniform application\r\nby economic operators in all Member States.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 19 February 2015\r\nCommission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2015/275[6]\r\nremoved those five deceased persons from Annex III to Regulation (EC) No\r\n314/2004.\n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Annex IV to Council\r\nRegulation (EU) No 314/2004 should be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nAnnex IV to Regulation (EC) No 314/2004 is\r\namended in accordance with the Annex to this Regulation.\nArticle 2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\r\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\r\nEuropean Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\r\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
