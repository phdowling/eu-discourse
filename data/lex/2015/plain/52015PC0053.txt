Since then it has been regularly extended for further periods of\r\ntwo years. The Agreement was extended last time by decision of the International\r\nSugar Council in June 2013 and remains into force until 31 December 2015.\nA further extension of the Agreement by up\r\nto two years is in the interest of the Union.\nThat extension of the Agreement entails the\r\nprolongation of the EU contribution to the administrative budget of the\r\nAgreement. That contribution is budgeted under item 05 06 01 of the\r\nEU budget (International agricultural agreements).\nThe purpose of this proposal is to seek the\r\nCouncil\u2019s authorisation to the Commission to vote, on behalf of the Union, in favour of the extension of the Agreement up to 31 December 2017 within the\r\nInternational Sugar Council.\n2015/0032 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\nestablishing the position to be taken on\r\nbehalf of the European Union within the International Sugar Council as regards\r\nthe extension of the International Sugar Agreement 1992\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 207 in conjunction\r\nwith Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the European\r\nCommission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The International Sugar Agreement 1992 was concluded by\r\nCouncil Decision 92/580/EEC[2]\r\nand entered into force on 1 January 1993 for a period of three years until 31\r\nDecember 1995. Since then, it has been regularly extended for further periods\r\nof two years. It was last extended by decision of the International Sugar\r\nCouncil in June 2013 and remains in force until 31 December 2015. A further\r\nextension is in the interest of the Union. The Commission, which represents the\r\n Union within the International Sugar Council, should therefore be authorised\r\nto vote in favour of such extension,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be taken on behalf of the Union within the International Sugar Council shall be to vote in favour of the extension of\r\nthe International Sugar Agreement 1992 for a further period of up to two years.\nThe Commission is hereby authorised to\r\nexpress that position within the International Sugar Council.\nArticle 2\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\r\nday of its adoption.\nDone at
