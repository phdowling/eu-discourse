"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52015PC0052\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION establishing the position to be taken on behalf of the European Union within the International Grains Council with respect to the extension of the Grains Trade Convention 1995 /* COM/2015/052 final - 2015/0031 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Grains Trade\r\nConvention 1995 (hereinafter: the \u201cConvention\u201d) was concluded by the Community\r\nby Council Decision 96/88/EC[1] until 30 June 1998 and, since then, it has\r\nbeen regularly extended. The Convention was last extended by decision of the\r\nInternational Grains Council in June 2013 and remains into force until 30 June\r\n2015.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A further extension of the\r\nConvention by up to two years is in the interest of the Union.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The extension of the\r\nConvention entails the prolongation of the EU contribution to the\r\nadministrative budget of the International Grains Council, which covers both\r\nthe Grains Trade Convention 1995 and the Food Aid Convention 1999. That\r\ncontribution is budgeted under item 05 06 01 of the EU budget\r\n(International agricultural agreements).\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The purpose of this\r\nproposal is to seek the Council\u2019s authorisation to the Commission to vote, on\r\nbehalf of the Union, in favour of the extension of the Grains Trade Convention\r\nup to 30 June 2017 within the International Grains Council.\n2015/0031 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\nestablishing the position to be taken on\r\nbehalf of the European Union within the International Grains Council with\r\nrespect to the extension of the Grains Trade Convention 1995\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 207 in conjunction\r\nwith Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the European\r\nCommission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Grains Trade Convention 1995 was concluded by Council\r\nDecision 96/88/EC[2]\r\nand was regularly extended for further periods of two years. That Convention\r\nwas extended last time by decision of the International Grains Council in June\r\n2013 and remains in force until 30 June 2015. A further extension is in the\r\ninterest of the Union. The Commission, which represents the Union within the International\r\nGrains Council, should therefore be authorised to vote in favour of such\r\nextension,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be taken on behalf of the Union within the International Grains Council shall be to vote in favour of the extension\r\nof the Grains Trade Convention 1995 for a further period of up to two years.\nThe Commission is hereby authorised to\r\nexpress that position within the International Grains Council.\nArticle\r\n2\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\r\nday of its adoption.\nDone at
