concerning amending the rules of procedure of the EEA Joint Committee (Croatia Enlargement) /* COM/2015/018 final - 2015/0011 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend the rules of\r\nprocedure of the EEA Joint Committee reflecting the Agreement on the\r\nparticipation of the Republic of Croatia in the European Economic Area (\u201cthe\r\n2014 EEA Enlargement Agreement\u201d), signed on 11 April 2014 in Brussels.\nThe 2014 EEA Enlargement Agreement has been\r\nprovisionally applicable to its signatories since 12 April 2014.\nThe 2014 EEA Enlargement Agreement amends\r\nArticle 129(1) of the EEA Agreement to add Croatian to the list of languages of\r\nthe EEA Agreement.\nThe rules of procedure of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee, which were adopted by Decision of the EEA Joint Committee No 1/94 of\r\n8 February 1994 and amended by Decision of the EEA Joint Committee No 24/2005\r\nof 8 February 2005[1],\r\nshould therefore be amended accordingly.\nThe Decision of the EEA Joint Committee is\r\nto apply provisionally pending the entry into force of the 2014 EEA Enlargement\r\nAgreement.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) (b) first indent of Council\r\nRegulation (EC) No 2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA\r\nAgreement provides that for the purpose of adopting the Union's position\r\nregarding EEA Joint Committee decisions other than those which extend Union\r\nlegislation to the EEA, the Council acts, on a proposal from the Commission, by\r\na simple majority when the decision relates to the Committee's Rules of\r\nProcedure or a question of procedure. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\r\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\r\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\r\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2015/0011 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\r\nof the European Union,\r\nwithin the EEA Joint Committee concerning amending the rules of procedure of\r\nthe EEA Joint Committee\r\n(Croatia Enlargement)\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, \nHaving regard to Council Regulation (EC) No\r\n2894/94 of 28 November 1994 concerning arrangements for implementing the\r\nAgreement on the European Economic Area[2],\r\nand in particular Article 1(3) (b) first indent thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The 129(1) of the EEA Agreement\r\nto add Croatian to the list of languages of the EEA Agreement.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The rules of procedure of\r\nthe EEA Joint Committee, which were
