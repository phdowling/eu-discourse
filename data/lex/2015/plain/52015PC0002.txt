The Agreement was signed on 15 December 2010. \n 120 || The Republic of Croatia acceded to the European Union on 1 July 2013. Pursuant to Article 6(2) of the Act concerning the Conditions of Accession, Croatia undertook to accede to the agreements concluded or signed by the Member States and the Union with one or more third countries or an international organisation. The simplified procedure applies to Croatia's accession to the above mentioned agreement with Jordan. A Protocol should thus be signed in accordance with that procedure and with Article 218(5) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union. The Protocol provides for the necessary linguistic adaptations of the Agreement that ensue from the accession of Croatia. \n 130 || \u00b7                   Existing provisions in the area covered by the proposal The provisions of the Protocol supersede or complement the provisions in the Euro-Mediterranean Aviation Agreement between the European Union, its Member States and the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. \n 140 || \u00b7                                            Consistency with other policies and objectives of the Union The Agreement with Jordan forms part of the Union's external aviation policy framework established by the Commission Communication COM(2005)79: \"Developing the agenda for the Community's external aviation policy\" and recently reviewed by the Commission Communication COM(2012)556: \"The EU's External Aviation Policy - Addressing Future Challenges\" and the corresponding Council Conclusions. \n 2)      Consultation of the stakeholders and impact assessment \n   || \u00b7                   Consultation of the stakeholders \n 211 || Consultation methods, main sectors targeted and general profile of respondents Not applicable \n 212 || Summary of responses and how they have been taken into account Not applicable \n 3)      LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE PROPOSAL \n 305 || \u00b7                   Summary of the proposed measures The Protocol ensures the necessary amendment to be made to the Euro-Mediterranean Aviation Agreement between the European Union, its Member States and the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan following the accession of Croatia to the EU on 1 July 2013. \n 310 || \u00b7                   Legal basis Article 100(2) and Article 218(5) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union in conjunction with Article 6(2) of the Act of Accession. \n 4)      Budgetary implications \n 409 || The proposal has no implications for the Union budget. \n 5)      ADDITIONAL INFORMATION \n 570 || \u00b7                   Detailed explanation of the proposal The Council is requested to approve the signing of the Protocol amending the Euro-Mediterranean Aviation Agreement between the European Union, its Member States and the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.
