The Agreement was signed on 26 June 2012. \n 120 || The Republic of Croatia acceded to the European Union on 1 July 2013. Pursuant to Article 6(2) of the Act concerning the Conditions of Accession, Croatia undertook to accede to the agreements concluded or signed by the Member States and the Union with one or more third counties or an international organisation. A Protocol had been negotiated, containing the necessary linguistic adaptations of the Agreement that ensue from the accession of Croatia. Under the above mentioned Article 6(2) as well as Articles 100(2) and 218(5) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, the signing of the Protocol has been approved by Decision of [\u2026], and the Protocol has been signed on [\u2026.] The Protocol should now be approved, on the basis of Articles 100(2) and 218(6)(a) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union, in conjunction with Article 6(2) of the Act of Accession. \n 130 || \u00b7                   Existing provisions in the area covered by the proposal The provisions of the Protocol supersede or complement the provisions in the Common Aviation Area Agreement between the European Union and its Member States and the Republic of Moldova. \n 140 || \u00b7                   Consistency with other policies and objectives of the Union The Agreement with Moldova forms part of the Union's external aviation policy framework established by the Commission Communication COM(2005)79: \"Developing the agenda for the Community's external aviation policy\" and recently reviewed by the Commission Communication COM(2012)556: \"The EU's External Aviation Policy - Addressing Future Challenges\" and the corresponding Council Conclusions. \n 2)      Consultation of the stakeholders and impact assessment \n \u00b7      || \u00b7                   Consultation of the stakeholders \n 211 || Consultation
