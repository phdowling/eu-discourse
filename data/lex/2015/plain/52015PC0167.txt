2015/0086 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\r\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\r\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\r\npossible after its adoption. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Annex II to\r\nthe EEA Agreement in order to incorporate the following in the EEA Agreement,\r\nand more concretly:\n(1)                   \r\nRegulation (EC) No 258/97 of the European\r\nParliament and of the Council of 27 January 1997 concerning novel foods and\r\nnovel food ingredients[1].\n(2)                   \r\nCommission Regulation (EC) No 1852/2001 of 20\r\nSeptember 2001 laying down detailed rules for making certain information\r\navailable to the public and for the protection of information submitted\r\npursuant to European Parliament and Council Regulation (EC) No 258/97[2].\n(3)                   \r\nCommission Recommendation 97/618/EC of 29 July 1997\r\nconcerning the scientific aspects and the presentation of information necessary\r\nto support applications for the placing on the market of novel foods and novel\r\nfood ingredients and the preparation of initial assessment reports under\r\nRegulation (EC) No 258/97 of the European Parliament and of the Council[3].\n(4)                   \r\nArticle 38 of Regulation (EC) No 1829/2003 of\r\nthe European Parliament and of the Council of 22 September 2003 on genetically\r\nmodified food and feed[4]\r\namends Regulation (EC) No 258/97.\nThe EEA EFTA side is proposing notably an\r\nadaptation to Article 7 of Regulation (EC) No 258/97. The proposed text states\r\nthat when the Commission takes authorization decisions under that Article, the\r\nEFTA States will simultaneously and within 30 days take corresponding decisions.\nAnnex II to the EEA agreement should\r\ntherefore be amended accordingly.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council\r\nRegulation (EC) No 2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA\r\nAgreement provides that the Council establishes the position to be adopted on\r\nthe Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the\r\nDraft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the\r\nUnion\u2019s position.
