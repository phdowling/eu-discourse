"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0596\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION modifying the Internal Agreement between the Representatives of the Governments of the Member States, meeting within the Council, on the financing of European Union aid under the multi-annual financial framework for the period 2014 to 2020, in accordance with the ACP-EU Partnership Agreement, and on the allocation of the financial assistance for the Overseas Countries and Territories to which part Four of the Treaty on the Functioning of the EU applies /* COM/2013/0596 final - 2013/0292 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nThe Internal Agreement between the\r\nRepresentatives of the Governments of the Member States, meeting within the\r\nCouncil, on the financing of European Union aid under the multi-annual\r\nfinancial framework for the period 2014 to 2020, in accordance with the ACP-EU\r\nPartnership Agreement, and on the allocation of the financial assistance for\r\nthe Overseas Countries and Territories to which part Four of the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the EU applies (hereinafter referred to as \u2018Internal Agreement\u2019)\r\nwas signed on 26 June 2013.\nThe Republic of Croatia acceded on 1 July 2013\r\nto the European Union[1]. According to the Joint Declaration C attached to the Act concerning\r\nthe conditions of accession of the Republic of Croatia, the Republic of Croatia will accede to the European Development Fund as of the entry into force of\r\nthe new Multiannual Financial Framework of Cooperation following its accession\r\nto the Union.\nAs regards the contribution of the Member\r\nStates to the eleventh European Development Fund (hereinafter referred to as\r\n'EDF'), Article 1(2)(a) of the Internal Agreement contains merely the estimated\r\ncontribution and contribution key for Croatia. It is stated in Article 1(7) of\r\nthe Internal Agreement that the amounts and contribution keys shall be amended\r\nby a decision of the Council acting unanimously on a proposal from the\r\nCommission, should a new State accede to the Union.\nRegarding the weighting of votes in the EDF Committee,\r\nArticle 8(2) of the Internal Agreement contains merely the estimated votes for Croatia. It is stated in Article 8(4) of the Internal Agreement that the weightings and the\r\nqualified majority shall be amended by a decision of the Council acting\r\nunanimously, should a new State accede to the Union.\nOn the aforementioned basis the estimated\r\ncontributions and votes for Croatia shall be confirmed by the attached draft\r\ndecision of the Council.\nThe Commission therefore suggests that the\r\nCouncil adopts the attached decision.\n2013/0292 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\nmodifying the Internal Agreement between\r\nthe Representatives of the Governments of the Member States, meeting within the\r\nCouncil, on the financing of European Union aid under the multi-annual\r\nfinancial framework for the period 2014 to 2020, in accordance with the ACP-EU\r\nPartnership
