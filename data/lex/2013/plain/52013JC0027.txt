JOIN/2013/027 final - 2013/0332 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)              \nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 267/2012[1] of 23 March 2012 gives effect\nto the measures provided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP concerning restrictive\nmeasures against Iran and provides for, inter alia, the freezing of all funds\nand economic resources belonging to, owned, held or controlled by the persons,\nentities and bodies listed in Annexes VIII and IX to the Regulation.\n(2)              \nOn \u2026 October 2013, the Council adopted Decision\n2013/\u2026/CFSP[2]\nwhich amends Council Decision 2010/413/CFSP and provides for an adjusted\nlisting criterion to include persons and entities evading or violating\nrestrictive measures.\n(3)              \nFurther action by the Union is needed in order\nto implement these measures.\n(4)              \nThe High Representative of the Union for Foreign\nAffairs and Security Policy and the European Commission propose to amend\nRegulation (EU) No 267/2012 accordingly.\n2013/0332 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Regulation (EU) No 267/2012\nconcerning restrictive measures against Iran\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof, \nHaving regard to Council Decision\n2010/413/CFSP of 26 July 2010 concerning restrictive measures against Iran and repealing Common Position 2007/140/CFSP [3],\nHaving regard to the joint proposal from\nthe High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy\nand the European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\n267/2012[4]\ngives effect to the measures provided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP and provides\nfor, inter alia, the freezing of all funds and economic resources belonging to,\nowned, held or controlled by the persons, entities and bodies listed in Annexes\nVIII and IX to the Regulation.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On \u2026 October 2013, the\nCouncil adopted Decision 2013/\u2026/CFSP[5]\nwhich amends Council Decision 2010/413/CFSP and provides for an adjusted\nlisting criterion to include persons and entities evading or violating\nrestrictive measures.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 That measure fall within\nthe scope of the Treaty and regulatory action at the level of the Union is therefore necessary in order to implement it, in particular with a view to\nensuring its uniform application by economic operators in all Member States.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Regulation (EU) No\n267/2012 should therefore be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nRegulation (EU) No 267/2012 is amended as\nfollows:\nIn Article 23(2), point (b), is replaced by the\nfollowing:\n\u2018(b) being a natural or legal person, entity or\nbody that has evaded or violated, or assisted a listed person, entity or body\nto evade or violate, the provisions of this Regulation, Council Decision\n2010/413/CFSP or UNSCR 1737 (2006), UNSCR 1747 (2007), UNSCR 1803 (2008) and\nUNSCR 1929 (2010);\u2019\nArticle 2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\nthe day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
