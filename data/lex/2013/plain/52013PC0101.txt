They take into account, in\nparticular, the limited laboratory capacity for pesticide residue analysis in\nIceland.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf\non such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2013/0061 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to
