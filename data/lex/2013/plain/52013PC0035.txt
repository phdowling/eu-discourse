Since the vast majority of\nmaintenance claims involve children, the Convention is first and foremost a\nmeasure to protect children.\nOn 31 March 2011, the Council adopted the decision on the signing, on behalf of the European\nUnion, of the 2007 Hague Maintenance Convention. The Convention was signed by\nthe European Union on 6 April 2011.\nOn 9 June 2011, the Council adopted the\ndecision on the approval, on behalf of the European Union, of the 2007 Hague Maintenance\nConvention. The deposit of the instrument of approval was foreseen after the\ndeadline of 10 December 2012 established by Article 7 of the Council Decision\nfor the notification to the Commission by the Member States of the contact\ndetails of the Central Authorithies designated under the Convention and the\ninformation concerning laws, procedures and services referred to in Article 57\nof the Convention.\nArticles 5 and 6 of the Council Decision\nprovide also for the Union to make the reservation under Article 44 (3) and the\nreservations under point (g) of Article 11(1) and Article 44 (1) and (2). The\ntext of the declaration and of the reservations is attached in Annex II and III\nto the Council Decision.\nDuring negotiations, the deadline of 16 May\n2011 was set in order to allow Member States to provide information for the\ndeclaration and reservations to be made by the Union at the time of the deposit\nof the instrument of approval.\nHowever, after the adoption of the Council\nDecision on 9 June 2011, some Member States have felt\nthe necessity to amend their previous statement (Latvia) or to make ex novo\nthe declaration and reservations under Article 5 and 6 of the Council Decision\n(Cyprus, Luxembourg and Portugal).\nBefore depositing the instrument of\napproval, Annexes II and III need therefore to be amended accordingly. \n\n2.                      \nRESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS WITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES \n\nThe issue was discussed in the Council during\nthe meetings of the Working Party on Civil Law Matters (General Questions), in\nparticular on 11 June 2012. The last requests of amendment of the annexes were\nreceived end of July 2012 and the English translations on 20 August 2012.
