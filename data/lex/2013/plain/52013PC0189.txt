"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0189\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION on the allocation of the fishing opportunities under the Protocol agreed between the European Union and the Republic of C\u00f4te d'Ivoire setting out fishing opportunities and the financial contribution provided for in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the two parties currently in force /* COM/2013/0189 final - 2013/0101 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nOn the basis\nof powers conferred on it by the Council[1],\nthe European Commission has conducted negotiations with the Republic of C\u00f4te\nd\u2019Ivoire with a view to renewing the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership\nAgreement between the European Community and the Republic of C\u00f4te d\u2019Ivoire. At\nthe end of those negotiations, a new draft Protocol was initialled by the\nnegotiators on 9 January 2013. The new Protocol covers a period of five years\nfrom 1 July 2013, the date of provisional application laid down in Article\n13.\nThe main aim\nof the Protocol to the Agreement is to provide fishing opportunities for EU\nvessels in the waters of the Republic of C\u00f4te d\u2019Ivoire within the limits of the\navailable surplus. The Commission's negotiating position was based in part on\nthe results of an ex-post evaluation carried out by external experts.\nThe general\naim is to enhance the cooperation between the European Union and the Republic\nof C\u00f4te d\u2019Ivoire, thereby creating a partnership framework within which to\ndevelop a sustainable fisheries policy and sound exploitation of fishery\nresources in the Ivorian fishing zone, in the interests of both Parties. \nMore particularly, the Protocol provides\nfor fishing opportunities in the following categories:\n\u2013     \n28 freezer tuna seiners: \n\u2013     \n10 surface longliners \nThe method for allocating these fishing\nopportunities among the Member States should be defined.\nThe Commission proposes, on this basis,\nthat the Council adopt this Regulation.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe interested parties were consulted\nduring the evaluation of the Protocol for the period 2007-13. Experts from the\nMember States were also consulted in technical meetings. These consultations\nconcluded that it would be beneficial to maintain a fishing protocol with the\nRepublic of C\u00f4te d\u2019Ivoire.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0
