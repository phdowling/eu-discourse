The Joint Committee has as\r\nits purpose, monitoring the development of the Agreement and intensifying the\r\nco-operation and dialogue on geographical indications between the EU and Moldova.\nFurther to Article 11(2) of the Agreement,\r\nthe Joint Committee shall determine its own rules of procedure. These rules of\r\nprocedure should be based on the draft Decision of the Joint Committee, which\r\nis annexed to the Decision. The proposed rules have already been agreed by the\r\nauthorities of the Republic of Moldova and follow the standard text, for this\r\ntype of agreement.\n2013/0300 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL the\r\nEuropean Union and Moldova on protection of geographical indications of\r\nagricultural products and foodstuffs, and in particular Article 11 thereof,\nWhereas that Agreement entered into force on\r\n1 April 2013,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION:\nArticle 1\r\nHeads of Delegation\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The European Union and Moldova (\"the Parties\"), shall each appoint a Head of Delegation who shall be the\r\ncontact person for all matters relating to the Committee.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Each Head of Delegation\r\nmay delegate all or any of the functions of Head of Delegation to a nominated\r\ndeputy, in which case all references hereafter to the Head of Delegation apply\r\nequally to the nominated deputy.\nArticle 2\r\nChair\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The office of Chair of the\r\nCommittee shall be held alternately, for a period of one calendar year, by the\r\nHead of Delegation of each Party.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Chair shall be\r\nresponsible for the secretarial duties of the Committee.\nArticle 3\r\nMeetings\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Chair shall fix the\r\ndate and venue or, in the case of meetings by electronic means, the technical\r\narrangements, of meetings in agreement with the other Head of Delegation. The\r\nChair and the other Head of Delegation shall, in agreeing the time and place of\r\nthe meeting, observe the requirement to hold a meeting within 90 days.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Where both Parties agree,\r\nJoint Committee meetings can be attended by experts that are able to supply\r\nrequested specific information.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Unless otherwise jointly\r\nagreed, the meetings of the Committee shall not be public.\nArticle 4\r\nCorrespondence\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 All correspondence to or for\r\nthe Committee shall be sent to the Chair of the Committee. The latter shall\r\nsend a copy of all correspondence relating to the Committee to the other Head\r\nof Delegation, to the Head of the Moldovan Mission in Brussels and to the Head\r\nof the Delegation of the EU in Chisinau.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Correspondence between the\r\nChair and the other Head of Delegation may be by any written means, including\r\nelectronic mail.\nArticle 5\r\nAgendas for the meetings\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Chair shall draw up\r\nthe draft agenda prior to a meeting. The draft agenda shall be sent to the\r\nother Head of Delegation no later than 20 working days before the start of the\r\nmeeting. The draft agenda circulated by the Chair shall include any item\r\ncovered by Article 11(3) of the Agreement, chosen by the Chair.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Heads of Delegation\r\nmay request additional items covered by Article 11(3) at least 10 working days\r\nbefore the start of the meeting, which the Chair must include on the draft\r\nagenda.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A final draft agenda shall\r\nbe circulated to the other Head of Delegation by the Chair at least five\r\nworking days before the start of the meeting.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The agenda shall be\r\nadopted by joint agreement by the Chair and the other Head of Delegation at the\r\nstart of each meeting. An item other than those appearing on the draft agenda\r\nmay be placed on the agenda if the Chair and other Head of Delegation so agree.\nArticle 6\r\nAdoption of instruments\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The decisions of the\r\nCommittee within the meaning of Article 11(2) of the Agreement shall be\r\naddressed to the Parties and shall bear the signatures of the Chair and of the\r\nother Head of Delegation.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Either Party may decide to\r\npublish any decision adopted by the Committee.\nArticle 7\r\nWritten procedure\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A decision of the\r\nCommittee may be adopted by a written procedure where the Chair and the other\r\nHead of Delegation so agree.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Head of Delegation\r\nproposing the use of the written procedure shall submit the draft decision to\r\nthe other Head of Delegation. The other Head of Delegation shall reply,\r\nindicating whether he or she accepts or does not accept the draft, proposes any\r\namendments, or requests further time for reflection. If the draft is adopted,\r\nit shall be finalised in accordance with Article 6(1).\nArticle 8\r\nMinutes\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Chair shall draw up\r\ndraft minutes of each meeting and submit them to the other Head of Delegation\r\nwithin 20 working days of the meeting. The draft minutes shall state the\r\nrecommendations made and may also note any other conclusions reached. The other\r\nHead of Delegation shall agree to the draft, or submit proposed amendments.\r\nOnce there is agreement on the draft minutes, two original copies shall be\r\nsigned by the Chair and by the other Head of Delegation. An original copy of\r\nthe minutes shall be kept by the Chair and one by the other Head of Delegation.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In the event there is no\r\nagreement on the minutes before the subsequent meeting is convened, the minutes\r\nshall record the draft drawn up by the Chair to which shall be annexed the\r\nproposed amendments submitted by the other Head of Delegation.\nArticle 9\r\nExpenses\nEach Party shall bear the expenses it\r\nincurs in taking part in the meetings of the Committee.\nArticle 10\r\nConfidentiality\nThe deliberations of the Committee shall be\r\nconfidential.\n[1]\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 OJ L10, 15 January 2013, p.1\n[2]\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 OJ L 61, 5 March 2013, p.1.\n\n\n\n\nTop  \n  "
