"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0543\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION extending the validity of Decision 2012/96/EU /* COM/2013/0543 final - 2013/0261 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nBy Council Decision 2012/470/EU of 7 August 2012, the validity of\r\nCouncil Decision 2012/96/EU was extended until 20 August 2013 and the\r\napplication of appropriate measures set out in Decision 2002/148/EC was\r\nsuspended. Given uncertainty as to the exact timing of when elections in line\r\nwith the Global Political Agreement will be held in Zimbabwe, and with a view\r\nto review appropriate measures immediately after the said elections, it is\r\nproposed to the Council to extend for a period of six months the validity of\r\nCouncil Decision 2012/96/EU by adopting the attached proposal.\n2013/0261 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\nextending the validity of Decision\r\n2012/96/EU\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union,\nHaving regard to the Partnership Agreement\r\nbetween the members of the African, Caribbean and Pacific Group of States, of\r\nthe one part, and the European Community and its Member States, of the other\r\npart, signed in Cotonou on 23 June 2000[1] (hereinafter referred to as the\r\n\u2018ACP-EC Partnership Agreement\u2019), as revised in Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso on 22\r\nJune 2010[2],\r\nand in particular Article 96 thereof,\nHaving regard to the Internal Agreement on\r\nmeasures to be taken and procedures to be followed for the implementation of\r\nthe ACP-EC Partnership Agreement[3],\r\nand in particular Article 3 thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nCommission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 By Decision 2002/148/EC,\r\nconsultations with the Republic of Zimbabwe under Article 96(2)(c) of the\r\nCotonou Agreement were concluded and appropriate measures taken. These measures\r\nhave since been adapted and their period of application reviewed each year.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 To demonstrate the Union\u2019s\r\ncontinued commitment to the political process under the Global Political\r\nAgreement, the Council on 7 August 2012 decided to extend the validity of\r\nDecision 2012/96/EU, and to suspend the application of the appropriate measures\r\nlimiting cooperation with Zimbabwe under Article 96 of the Cotonou Agreement\r\nfor a period of 12 months.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In line with the Council\u2019s\r\nagreement to review appropriate measures immediately after the holding of\r\nelections in Zimbabwe in accordance with the Global Political Agreement, Decision\r\n2012/96/EU should be extended, while maintaining the suspension of the\r\nappropriate measures.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The European Union can decide\r\nto review this decision at any time,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION:\nArticle 1\nThe validity of Decision 2012/96/EU and of\r\nits appropriate measures is hereby extended until 20 February 2014. However,\r\nthe application of the appropriate measures remains suspended.\nThe appropriate measures shall be kept\r\nunder constant review and shall be applied again if the situation in Zimbabwe is to seriously deteriorate.\nSuch measures shall in any event be\r\nreviewed six months after the entry into force of this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\r\ndate of its adoption.
