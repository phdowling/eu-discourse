"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0335\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the position to be adopted, on behalf of the European Union, in the EEA Joint Committee amending Protocol 30 to the EEA Agreement, on specific provisions on the organization of cooperation in the field of statistics /* COM/2013/0335 final - 2013/0175 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 30.\nThe EEA EFTA States aim to continue their participation in the EU multiannual\nstatistical programme. As the financial envelope of the European statistical\nprogramme 2013-17 has been set for 2013 only, and although the financial\nframework for 2014 to 2017 remains to be decided upon, participation in the\nprogramme for 2013 should already be decided upon by the EEA Joint Committee.\nThus, the draft JCD incorporates the new\nEuropean statistical programme 2013-17 into Protocol 30 to the EEA Agreement\nfor the year 2013. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf\non such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2013/0175 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union,\nin the EEA Joint
