For each Member State, an alternate should be appointed\nfor each category of representative. The representatives and alternates are\nappointed by the Council, acting on a proposal from the Commission for a period\nof three years.\nBy Decision 2010/C 53/02 of 22 February 2010,\nthe Council decided to appoint the members and alternate members of the\nCommittee for the period from 1 January 2010 to 31 December 2012. \nIn view of the nominations submitted by the\nMember States in accordance with Article 104(2) of Regulation (EC) No 1083/2006,\nthe Commission submits to the Council the present proposal for a Council\nDecision appointing members and alternate members of the European Social Fund\nCommittee for the period from 1 January 2013 to 31 December 2013.\n2013/0447 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\nappointing members of the European Social\nFund Committee \nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 163 thereof,\nHaving regard to Regulation (EC) No\n1083/2006 of 11 July 2006 laying down general provisions on the European\nRegional Development Fund, the European Social Fund and the Cohesion Fund and\nrepealing Regulation (EC) No 1260/1999[2],\nand in
