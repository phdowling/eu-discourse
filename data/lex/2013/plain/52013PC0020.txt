"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0020\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the submission, on behalf of the European Union, of applications for new entries in Annex VIII or IX to the Basel Convention on the control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes and their disposal /* COM/2013/020 final - 2013/0011 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Basel Convention on\nthe Control of Transboundary Movements of Hazardous Wastes and Their Disposal,\ndone on 22 March 1989 (the \"Basel Convention\"), establishes a control\nprocedure for the export and import of hazardous wastes between Parties. The\nConvention entered into force in 1992 and now binds 175 Parties. The EU is a\nParty to the Basel Convention.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The procedure for amending\nannexes of the Basel Convention is governed by Articles 17 and 18 of the\nConvention. More particularly, any proposed amendment must be made by a Party\nand communicated by the Secretariat to all Parties at least six months before\nthe meeting at which it is proposed for adoption. In addition, any such\namendment must be adopted at a meeting of the Conference of the Parties and may\nbecome effective six months from the date of issuance of a circular\nnotification by the Depositary.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Applications for new\nentries in Annex VIII or IX to the Basel Convention are governed by the\nprocedure agreed at the 8th meeting of the Conference of the Parties\nto the Basel Convention under Decision VIII/15 \"Revisions to the procedure\nfor the review or adjustment of the lists of wastes contained in Annexes VIII\nand IX.\"\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 58 of Regulation\n(EC) No 1013/2006 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 14 June 2006\non shipments of waste[1],\nthe annexes to this Regulation may be amended in accordance with the procedure\nestablished by Article 39 of Directive 2008/98/EC of the European parliament\nand of the Council[2]\nto take account of scientific and technical progress. According to Article\n58(1) of Regulation (EC) No 1013/2006, wastes are added in Annex IIIA, IIIB, IV\nor V on a provisional basis pending a decision on their inclusion in the\nrelevant annexes to the Basel Convention or to Decision C(2001)107/Final of the\nOECD Council concerning the revision of Decision C(92)39/Final on control of\ntransboundary movements of wastes destined for recovery operations.\n5.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Following an amendment of\nAnnex IIIA, IIIB, IV or V to Regulation (EC) No 1013/2006, it is necessary, in\norder to ensure consistency, to submit, on behalf of the European Union, the\nnecessary applications for corresponding entries in Annex VIII or IX to the\nBasel Convention on the control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes\nand their disposal. \n6.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 According to the procedure\nagreed at the 8th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the\nBasel Convention under Decision VIII/15, any proposed amendment must be\nmade by a Party and communicated by the Secretariat to all Parties at least six\nmonths before the meeting at which it is proposed for adoption. Any such\namendment must be adopted at a meeting of the Conference of the Parties and may\nbecome effective six months from the date of issuance of a circular\nnotification by the Depositary.\n2013/0011 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the submission, on behalf of the\nEuropean Union, of applications for new entries in Annex VIII or IX to the\nBasel Convention on the control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes\nand their disposal\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 191(1), in\nconjunction with Article 218(9), thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 As a Party to the Basel\nConvention on the control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes and\ntheir disposal, the European Union may make proposals for amendments to annexes\nto the Basel Convention.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Applications for new\nentries in Annex VIII or IX to the Basel Convention are governed by the\nprocedure agreed at the 8th meeting of the Conference of the Parties\nto the Basel Convention under Decision VIII/15 \"Revisions to the procedure\nfor the review or adjustment of the lists of wastes contained in Annexes VIII\nand IX\".\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Following an amendment of\nAnnex IIIA, IIIB, IV or V to Regulation (EC) No 1013/2006 of the European\nParliament and of the Council of 14 June 2006 on shipments of waste[3], it appears necessary to ensure\nconsistency with Annex VIII or IX of the Basel Convention by amending them\naccordingly.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Union should therefore\nsubmit the necessary applications for those entries in Annex IIIA, IIIB, IV or\nV to Regulation (EC) No 1013/2006 which can be included in Annex VIII or IX to\nthe Basel Convention.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 With a view to the meeting\nof the Parties to the Basel Convention at which an amendment of Annex VIII or\nIX to the Convention is proposed for adoption, it is appropriate that the Union\nsubmits to the Secretariat of the Basel Convention the necessary applications\nfor new entries in these annexes at least six months before such meeting.\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nFollowing an amendment which adds new\nentries in Annex IIIA, IIIB, IV or V to Regulation (EC) No 1013/2006 that can\nbe included in Annex VIII or IX to the Basel Convention on the control of\ntransboundary movements of hazardous wastes and their disposal , the Union\nshall submit the necessary applications under the Convention.\nArticle 2\nThe Commission shall communicate these\napplications to the Secretariat of the Basel Convention at least six months\nbefore the next meeting of the Parties to this Convention.\nDone at
