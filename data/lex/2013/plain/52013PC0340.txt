concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2013/0340 final - 2013/0178 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal security\nand homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to integrate\nall the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as possible after\nits adoption and also allow for the participation of the EEA EFTA States in EEA\nrelevant EU actions or programmes.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee\n(annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement\non cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms in order to allow\nfor the EEA EFTA States\u2019 continued participation in EU actions funded from the\nGeneral Budget of the Union regarding the implementation, operation and\ndevelopment of the Internal Market. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf\non such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision of\nthe EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2013/0178 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union, in the EEA Joint Committee concerning an amendment to\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the\nfour freedoms\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 114, in conjunction\nwith Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 of 28 November 1994 concerning arrangements for implementing the\nAgreement on the European Economic Area[1],\nand in particular Article 1(3) thereof, \nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Agreement on the\nEuropean Economic Area[2]\n(\u201cthe EEA Agreement\u201d) entered into force on 1st January 1994.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 98 of\nthe Agreement, the EEA Joint Committee may decide to amend, among others,\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement\ncontains provisions and arrangements concerning cooperation in specific fields\noutside the four freedoms.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is appropriate to\ncontinue the cooperation of the Contracting Parties to the EEA Agreement in European\nUnion actions funded from the General Budget of the European Union regarding\nthe implementation, operation and development of the Internal Market.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement\nshould therefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to\ncontinue beyond 31 December 2012.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The position of the European\nUnion in the EEA Joint Committee should therefore be based on the attached\ndraft Decision,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be taken by the European\nUnion in the EEA Joint Committee on the proposed amendment to Protocol 31 to\nthe EEA Agreement shall be based on the draft Decision of that Committee,\nattached to this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThis\nDecision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption. \nDone at Brussels,
