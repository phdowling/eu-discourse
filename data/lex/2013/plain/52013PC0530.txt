"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0530\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the signing, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, and provisional application of the Protocol to the Agreement on Cooperation and Customs Union between the European Community and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of San Marino, of the other part, regarding the participation, as a contracting party, of the Republic of Croatia, following its accession to the European Union /* COM/2013/0530 final - 2013/0254 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nThe Republic of Croatia (hereinafter 'Croatia') will join the European Union on 1 July 2013. On 14 September 2012, the Council authorized\r\nthe Commission to negotiate the adaptation, through a Protocol, of the\r\nAgreement on Cooperation and Customs Union between the European Community and\r\nits Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of San Marino (hereinafter\r\n'San Marino'), of the other part[1]\r\n(hereinafter 'the Agreement'), regarding Croatia's participation as a\r\ncontracting party, in view of the enlargement of the Union to include the\r\nlatter.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nThe Agreement entered into force on 1 April 2002 and needs to be\r\namended to include Croatia as a contracting party. Following the authorisation\r\ngiven to the Commission on 14 September 2012, negotiations have been concluded\r\nwith San Marino on a Protocol to the Agreement.\nSummary of the proposed action\nThis proposal consists of a draft Council\r\nDecision on the signing, on behalf of the European\r\nUnion and its Member States, and provisional\r\napplication of a Protocol to the Agreement (in\r\nannex), to provide for Croatia's\r\nparticipation as a contracting party following its accession to the European\r\nUnion.\nLegal basis\nThe Treaty on the Functioning of the\r\nEuropean Union, and in particular Article 218(5), in conjunction with Article\r\n218(3) and (4) thereof; the Treaty of Accession of Croatia[2]; and the Act of Accession of\r\nCroatia[3],\r\nin particular the second subparagraph of Article 6(2) thereof.\nChoice of instrument\nPursuant to Article 218(5) of the Treaty on\r\nthe Functioning of the European Union, the Council shall adopt a Decision\r\nauthorising the signing of the Agreement, and if necessary, its provisional\r\napplication before entry into force. This is a proposal for such a Decision.\nProportionality\nThis proposal is in line with the principle\r\nof proportionality because its effects are strictly limited to what is\r\nnecessary to provide for Croatia's participation in the Agreement as a\r\ncontracting party following its accession to the European Union.\n2013/0254 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the signing, on behalf of the European\r\nUnion and its Member States, and provisional application of the Protocol to the\r\nAgreement on Cooperation and Customs Union between the European Community and\r\nits Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of San Marino, of the\r\nother part, regarding the participation, as a contracting party, of the\r\nRepublic of Croatia, following its accession to the European Union\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, in particular Article 207 and Article 352,\r\nin conjunction with Article 218(5) and the second subparagraph of Article\r\n218(8) thereof,\nHaving regard to the Act of Accession of\r\nCroatia[4],\r\nand in particular the second subparagraph of Article 6(2) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 14 September 2012, the\r\nCouncil authorised the Commission to open negotiations with the Republic of San\r\nMarino for the adaptation, through the negotiation of a Protocol, of the\r\nAgreement on Cooperation and Customs Union between the European Community and\r\nits Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of San Marino, of the\r\nother part[5],\r\nregarding Croatia's participation as a contracting party, in view of its\r\naccession to the European Union.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The\r\nnegotiations on the Protocol to the Agreement were conducted by the Commission\r\nand were recently concluded.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Protocol should be\r\nsigned on behalf of the European Union, subject to its conclusion at a later\r\ndate. \n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In view of Croatia's forthcoming accession to the Union and to ensure its participation as a contracting party to\r\nthe Agreement from its date of accession, the Protocol to the Agreement should\r\nbe applied provisionally from the said date.\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe signing on behalf of the Union and its\r\nMember States of the Protocol to the Agreement on Cooperation and Customs Union\r\nbetween the European Community and the Republic of San Marino, regarding\r\nCroatia's participation as a contracting party, following its accession to the\r\nEuropean Union, is hereby authorized, subject to the conclusion of the said\r\nProtocol.\nThe text of the Protocol is attached to\r\nthis Decision.\nArticle 2\nThe Council Secretariat-General shall\r\nestablish the instrument of full powers to sign the Protocol, subject to its\r\nconclusion, for the person(s) indicated by the negotiator.\nArticle 3\nSubject to its conclusion at a later date\r\nand pending its entry into force, the Protocol shall be applied provisionally,\r\nin accordance with its Article 5, as from the date of accession of Croatia to the Union.\nArticle 4\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\r\nday of its adoption.\nDone at Brussels,
