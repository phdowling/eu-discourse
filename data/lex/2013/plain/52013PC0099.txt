concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement /* COM/2013/099 final - 2013/0056 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption.\nArt. 78 of the EEA Agreement provides that\nthe Contracting Parties shall strengthen and broaden cooperation in the\nframework of the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, social policy.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31\nto the EEA Agreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four\nfreedoms in order to include cooperation concerning free movement of workers,\ncoordination of social security systems and measures for migrants, including\nmigrants from third countries.\nThis concerns participation of EFTA EEA\nstates in the actions funded from the following budget lines, entered in the\ngeneral budget of the European Union for the financial year 2013: \n- Budget line 04 01 04 08: \u201cFree\nmovement of workers, coordination of social security systems and measures for\nmigrants, including migrants from third countries \u2013 Expenditure on\nadministrative management\u201d, \n- Budget\nline 04 03 05: \u201cFree movement of workers, coordination of social security\nsystems and measures for migrants, including migrants from third countries\u201d \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf\non such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2013/0056 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union,\nin the EEA Joint Committee\nconcerning an amendment to Protocol 31\nto the EEA Agreement\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\nof the European Union, and in particular Articles- 46 and 48, in conjunction with Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 of 28 November 1994 concerning arrangements for implementing the\nAgreement on the European Economic Area[1],\nand in particular Article 1(3) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Agreement on the\nEuropean Economic Area[2]\n(\u201cthe EEA Agreement\u201d) entered into force on 1 January 1994.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 98 of\nthe EEA Agreement, the EEA Joint Committee may decide to amend, inter alia, Protocol\n31 thereto.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement contains specific provisions and arrangements concerning cooperation\nin specific fields outside the four freedoms. \n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is appropriate to extend\nthe cooperation of the Contracting Parties to the EEA Agreement to the actions\nfunded from the 04 01 04 08 and 04 03 05 budget lines for the financial year 2013.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement should therefore be amended accordingly.\n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The position of the Union\nin the EEA Joint Committee should be based on the attached draft Decision,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be adopted, on behalf of\nthe European Union, in the EEA Joint Committee on the proposed amendment to Protocol\n31 to the EEA Agreement shall be based on the draft Decision of the\nEEA Joint Committee attached to this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\nday of its adoption.\nDone at Brussels,
