"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013JC0025\n\nJoint Proposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION amending Regulation (EC) No 329/2007 concerning restrictive measures against the Democratic People\u2019s Republic of Korea /* JOIN/2013/025 final - 2013/0230 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)                   \nCouncil Regulation (EC) No 329/2007 of 27 March\n2007 gives effect to measures provided for in Council Decision 2010/800/CFSP of\n22 December 2010 concerning restrictive measures against the Democratic\nPeople's Republic of Korea (\"North Korea\"). On 22 April 2013 the\nCouncil adopted Decision 2013/183/CFSP repealing and replacing Decision\n2010/800/CFSP.\n(2)                   \nPursuant to Decision 2013/183/CFSP the Union shall take the necessary measures in order to determine the relevant items to be\nincluded in the lists of goods referred to in Articles 2 and 3 of Regulation\n329/2007. The list, set out in Annex Ia to that Regulation, should be expanded\nto include further items.\n(3)                   \nThe High Representative for Foreign Affairs and\nSecurity Policy and the Commission should therefore propose to amend Regulation\n(EC) No 329/2007 accordingly. \n2013/0230 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Regulation (EC) No 329/2007\nconcerning restrictive measures against the Democratic People\u2019s Republic of Korea\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on European Union, and in\nparticular Article 215 (1), thereof,\nHaving regard\nto Council Decision 2013/183/CFSP concerning restrictive measures against the\nDemocratic People\u2019s Republic of Korea and repealing Decision 2010/800/CFSP[1].\nHaving regard to the\njoint proposal from the High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs\nand Security Policy and the European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Annex I to Regulation (EC)\nNo 329/2007 lists all items, materials, equipment, goods and technology,\nincluding software, which are dual-use items or technology as defined in\nRegulation (EC) No 428/2009 of 5 May 2009[2]\nwhich are prohibited to be sold, supplied, transferred, or exported, to any\nperson, entity or body in, or for use in, the Democratic People\u2019s Republic of\nKorea ('North Korea') in accordance with Article 2 of Regulation (EC) No\n329/2007.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Annex Ia lists other\nitems, materials, equipment, goods and technology which could contribute to North Korea's nuclear-related, other weapons of mass destruction-related or ballistic\nmissile-related programmes which are also prohibited to be sold, supplied,\ntransferred or exported. It is for the Union to take the necessary measures in\norder to determine the relevant items to be included in that Annex.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The list of items in Annex Ia to Regulation (EC) No 329/2007 should be amended to add the list of items in Annex\nI to this Regulation. \n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Regulation (EU) No\n329/2007 should therefore be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle\n1\nRegulation No\n329/2007 is amended as follows:\nAnnex Ia to Regulation (EU)\nNo 329/2007 is amended in accordance with the text set\nout in the Annex to this Regulation.\nArticle\n2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on the date of its\npublication in the Official Journal of the European Union.\nThis Regulation is binding in its entirety and directly applicable\nin all Member States.\nDone at Brussels,
