"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0650\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION on the allocation of the fishing opportunities under the Protocol agreed between the European Union and the Kingdom of Morocco setting out fishing opportunities and the financial contribution provided for in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the two Parties currently in force /* COM/2013/0650 final - 2013/0317 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nOn the basis\r\nof a mandate from the Council[1],\r\nthe European Commission has opened negotiations with the Kingdom of Morocco on the renewal of the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European\r\nCommunity and the Kingdom of Morocco. At the end of the negotiations a new\r\ndraft Protocol was initialled by the negotiators on 24 July 2013. The new\r\nProtocol covers a period of four years from its entry into force. \nThe main aim\r\nof the Protocol to the Agreement is to provide fishing opportunities for\r\nEuropean Union vessels in the waters of the Kingdom of Morocco within the\r\nlimits of the available surplus. The Commission\u2019s negotiating position was\r\nbased in part on the results of an ex-post evaluation carried out by external\r\nexperts. \nThe general\r\naim is to strengthen cooperation between the European Union and the Kingdom of Morocco, thereby creating a partnership framework within which to develop a\r\nsustainable fisheries policy and sound exploitation of fisheries resources in\r\nthe Moroccan fishing zone, in the interests of both Parties. \nMore specifically, the\r\nProtocol provides for fishing opportunities in the following categories:\n\u2013     \r\nSmall-scale pelagic fishing in the north: 20\r\nseiners,\n\u2013     \r\nSmall-scale fishing in the north: 35 bottom\r\nlongliners,\n\u2013     \r\nSmall-scale fishing in the south: 10 vessels\r\n(rods and lines),\n\u2013     \r\nDemersal fishing: 16 bottom longliners and\r\nbottom trawls, \n\u2013     \r\nTuna fishing: 27 pole-and-line vessels\n\u2013     \r\nIndustrial pelagic fishing: 80 000 tonnes of\r\ncatch, 18 vessels \nThe method for allocating fishing\r\nopportunities among the Member States should be defined.\nThe Commission proposes, on this basis,\r\nthat the Council adopt the Regulation.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe interested parties were consulted\r\nduring the evaluation of the Protocol for the period 2007-11. Experts from the\r\nMember States were also consulted in technical meetings. These consultations\r\nled to the conclusion that it would be beneficial to maintain a fishing\r\nprotocol with the Kingdom of Morocco.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nThis procedure\r\nis being initiated in parallel with the procedures relating to the Council\r\nDecision for the signature of the Protocol, as well as the Council Decision for\r\nthe conclusion of the Protocol itself. \n2013/0317 (NLE)\nProposition de\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\non the allocation of the fishing\r\nopportunities under the Protocol agreed between the European Union and the\r\nKingdom of Morocco setting out fishing opportunities and the financial\r\ncontribution provided for in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the\r\ntwo Parties currently in force\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 43(3) thereof,\r\n\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 22 May 2006 the Council\r\nadopted Regulation (EC) No 764/2006 on the conclusion of the Fisheries\r\nPartnership Agreement between the European Community and the Kingdom of Moroc  co[2].\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The European Community and\r\nthe Kingdom of Morocco notified each other on 28 February 2007 of the\r\ncompletion of the procedures necessary for the entry into force of the\r\nPartnership Agreement between the European Union and the Kingdom of Morocco[3].\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A new Protocol to the\r\nPartnership Agreement was initialled on 24 July 2013 (hereinafter referred to\r\nas the 'new Protocol').
