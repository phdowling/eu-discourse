"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0060\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DIRECTIVE adapting certain directives in the field of right of establishment and freedom to provide services, by reason of the accession of Croatia /* COM/2013/060 final - 2013/0039 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nThe proposal for a Council Directive adapting\ncertain directives in the field of right of establishment and freedom to\nprovide services is necessitated by the upcoming accession of the Republic of   Croatia to the European Union.\nThe Treaty concerning the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union[1]\nwas signed by all Member States of the European Union and the Republic of Croatia at Brussels on 9 December 2011.\nArticle 3 (3) of the Treaty of Accession\nprovides that it shall enter into force on 1 July 2013 provided that all the\ninstruments of ratification have been deposited before that date.\nArticle 3(4) of the Treaty of Accession enables\nthe institutions of the Union to adopt before accession measures referred to,\ninter alia, in Article 50 of the Act concerning the conditions of accession of\nthe Republic of Croatia[2].\nThese measures shall enter into force only subject to and on the date of the\nentry into force of the Treaty of Accession.\nArticle 50 of the Act of Accession provides that where acts of the institutions adopted prior to\naccession require adaptation by reason of accession, and the necessary\nadaptations have not been provided for in this Act or its Annexes, the Council\nor the Commission (if the original acts were adopted by the Commission) shall\nadopt the necessary acts. \nPoint 2 of the Final Act[3] refers\nto the political agreement on a set of adaptations to be adopted by the\ninstitutions which was reached between the Member States and Croatia in the\ncontext of the approval of the Treaty of Accession; the High Contracting\nParties of the Treaty of Accession invited the Council and the Commission to\nadopt these adaptations before accession in accordance with Article 50 of the\nAct of Accession, completed and updated where necessary to take account of the\nevolution of the law of the Union.\nThe present proposal covers all Council\ndirectives as well as European Parliament and Council directives which require\ntechnical adaptation by reason of the accession of Croatia in the field right\nof establishment and freedom to provide services \u2013 corresponding to negotiation\nchapter 3.\nThis proposal is part of a series of proposals\nfor Council directives by the Commission to the Council which regroup the technical\nadaptations to Council directives as well as to European Parliament and Council\ndirectives corresponding to negotiation chapters into separate proposals for\ndifferent Council directives. This structure is designed to facilitate the\ntransposition of the directives concerned by Member States into their\nrespective legal orders. The package of proposals for legal acts which the\nCommission has transmitted to the Council is composed of this series of\nproposals for Council directives on the one hand, as well as of a proposal for\na single Council regulation which covers the relevant European Parliament and\nCouncil regulations and decisions as well as the relevant Council regulations\nand decisions on the other hand. This is in line with the approach which was\ntaken in the past in view of the accession of Bulgaria and Romania[4].\nIt is foreseen that all the legal acts included\nin this package will be published in the Official Journal of the European\nUnion at the same date.\nThe present proposal and the other proposals\nincluded in this package will take into account technical adaptations to the acquis\nwhich were published in the Official Journal of the European Union until 1\nSeptember 2012. The reasons for this are to provide sufficient time for the\nlegislative processes involved on the one hand, and for the fulfilment of the\nensuing transposition and notification obligations by Member States as regards\ndirectives on the other hand. Adaptations which may be necessary to the acquis\nwhich were published in the Official Journal of the European Union after 1\nSeptember 2012 will be foreseen in the relevant acts themselves or done at a\nlater stage through the appropriate procedure. In addition, the Commission\nintends to informally provide a list of such legislation to Member States in\nearly July 2013.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nAs this proposal is of a purely technical\nnature and does not involve any political choices, consultations with\ninterested parties or impact assessments would not have made sense.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nThe legal basis for the proposal is Article 50\nof the Act concerning the conditions of accession of the Republic of Croatia. \nThe principles of subsidiarity and\nproportionality are fully respected. The action of the Union is necessary under\nthe principle of subsidiarity (Article 5 (3) TEU) because it concerns technical\nadaptations to legal acts which were enacted by the Union. The proposal\nrespects the principle of proportionality (Article 5 (4) TEU) because it does\nnot go beyond what is necessary to reach the objective pursued.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 BUDGETARY IMPLICATION\nThe proposal has no budgetary implications.\n2013/0039 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DIRECTIVE\nadapting certain directives in the field\nof right of establishment and freedom to provide services, by reason of the\naccession of Croatia\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European\nUnion,\nHaving regard to the Treaty of Accession of the Republic of Croatia, and in particular Article 3(4) thereof,\nHaving regard to the Act of Accession of the Republic of Croatia, and in particular Article 50 thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 50 of\nthe Act of Accession, where acts of the institutions adopted prior to accession\nrequire adaptation by reason of accession, and the necessary adaptations have\nnot been provided for in the Act of Accession or its Annexes, the Council,\nacting by qualified majority on a proposal from the Commission, shall, to this\nend, adopt the necessary acts, if the original act was not adopted by the\nCommission.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Final Act of the\nConference which drew up the Treaty of Accession indicated that the High\nContracting Parties had reached political agreement on a set of adaptations to\nacts adopted by the institutions required by reason of accession and invited\nthe Council and the Commission to adopt these adaptations before accession,\ncompleted and updated where necessary to take account of the evolution of the\nlaw of the Union.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Directives 74/557/EEC[5], 77/249/EEC[6], 98/5/EC[7] and 2005/36/EC[8] should therefore be amended\naccordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DIRECTIVE:\nArticle 1\nDirectives 74/557/EEC, 77/249/EEC, 98/5/EC and 2005/36/EC shall be\namended as set out in the Annex.\nArticle 2\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Member States shall adopt\nand publish, by the date of accession of the Republic of Croatia to the\nEuropean Union at the latest, the laws, regulations and administrative\nprovisions necessary to comply with this Directive. They shall forthwith\ncommunicate to the Commission the text of those provisions.\nThey shall apply those provisions from the date\nof accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union.\nWhen Member States adopt those provisions, they\nshall contain a reference to this Directive or be accompanied by such a\nreference on the occasion of their official publication. Member States shall\ndetermine how such reference is to be made.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Member States shall\ncommunicate to the Commission the text of the main provisions of national law\nwhich they adopt in the field covered by this Directive.\nArticle 3\nThis Directive shall enter into force subject to, and as from the\ndate of the entry into force of the Treaty of Accession of the Republic of   Croatia.\nArticle 4\nThis Directive is addressed to the\nMember States.\nDone at Brussels, \n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 For\nthe Council\n\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The\nPresident\nANNEX \nRight of establishment and freedom\nto provide services\nI.
