Further negotiating rounds took place on 25 January and 10 April 1013. The\r\nGovernment of the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia confirmed its agreement with the protocol on 25 October 2013. The\r\nProtocol is updated to reflect the 2013 EU Customs Tariff and the gradual\r\nabolition of duties under the SAA. The text of the Protocol is attached.\nThe Commission proposes that the Council\r\ndecides on the signature and provisional application of the Protocol on behalf\r\nof the European Union and concludes the Protocol on the behalf of the European\r\nUnion and its Member States. For the conclusion of a Protocol on behalf of the\r\nEuropean Atomic Energy Community, the Commission proposes that the Council\r\ngives its approval, pursuant to the second paragraph of Article 101 of the\r\nTreaty establishing the EAEC. \nThe attached proposal is for a Council\r\nDecision on conclusion of the Protocol.
