situation in Libya /* JOIN/2013/029 final - 2013/0430 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)                   \nOn 2 March 2011 the Council adopted Regulation\n(EU) No 204/2011 concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Libya to give effect to the measures provided for in Decision 2011/137/CFSP of 28 February\n2011.\n(2)                   \nIt is necessary to amend the no claims clause\nand the non-liability clause in line with the wording of the Guidelines on the\nimplementation and evaluation of restrictive measures (sanctions) in the\nframework of the EU Common Foreign and Security Policy adopted by the Council\non 15 June 2012.\n(3)                   \nThe High Representative of the Union for Foreign\nAffairs and Security Policy and the European Commission propose to amend\nRegulation (EU) No 204/2011 accordingly.\n2013/0430 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Regulation (EU) No 204/2011\nconcerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Libya\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Decision 2011/137/CFSP\nof 28 February 2011 concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in\nLibya[1],\nHaving regard to the joint proposal from\nthe High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy\nand the European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\n204/2011 of 2 March 2011 concerning restrictive measures in view of the\nsituation in Libya[2]\ngives effect to the measures provided for in Decsion 2011/137/CFSP.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is necessary to amend\nthe no claims clause and the non-liability clause in line with the wording of\nthe Guidelines on the implementation and evaluation of restrictive measures\n(sanctions) in the framework of the EU Common Foreign and Security Policy\nadopted by the Council on 15 June 2012.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Regulation (EU) No\n204/2011 should therefore be amended accordingly, \nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nRegulation (EU) No 204/2011 is amended as\nfollows:\n(1)                   \nArticle 11 is replaced by the following:\n\u201cArticle 11\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The freezing of funds and\neconomic resources or the refusal to make funds or economic resources\navailable, carried out in good faith on the basis that such action is in\naccordance with this Regulation, shall not give rise to liability of any kind\non the part of the natural or legal person, entity or body implementing it, or\nits directors or employees, unless it is proved that the funds and economic\nresources were frozen or withheld as a result of negligence.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Actions by natural or legal\npersons, entities or bodies shall not give rise to liability of any kind on\ntheir part, if they did not know, and had no reasonable cause to suspect, that\ntheir actions would infringe the measures set out in this Regulation.\u201d\n(2)                   \nArticle 12 is replaced by the following:\n\u201cArticle 12\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 No claims in connection with any\ncontract or transaction the performance of which has been affected, directly or\nindirectly, in whole or in part, by the measures imposed under this Regulation,\nincluding claims for indemnity or any other claim of this type, such as a claim\nfor compensation or a claim under a guarantee, notably a claim for extension or\npayment of a bond, guarantee or indemnity, particularly a financial guarantee\nor financial indemnity, of whatever form, shall be satisfied, if they are made\nby: \n(a)         \ndesignated persons, entities or bodies listed in\nAnnex II or III;\n(b)         \nany other Libyan person, entity or body,\nincluding the Libyan government;\n(c)         \nany person, entity or body acting through or on\nbehalf of one of the persons, entities or bodies referred to in points (a) or\n(b).\n2. \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In any proceedings for the\nenforcement of a claim, the onus of proving that satisfying the claim is not\nprohibited by paragraph 1 shall be on the person seeking the enforcement of\nthat claim.\n3. \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 This Article is without prejudice\nto the right of the persons, entities and bodies referred to in paragraph 1 to\njudicial review of the legality of the non-performance of contractual\nobligations in accordance with this Regulation.\u201d\nArticle 2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\nEuropean Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
