"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0764\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION on the allocation of fishing opportunities under the Protocol agreed between the European Union and the Union of the Comoros setting out the fishing opportunities and financial contribution provided for in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the two parties currently in force /* COM/2013/0764 final - 2013/0382 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nOn the basis\r\nof the powers conferred on it by the Council[1],\r\nthe European Commission has conducted negotiations with the Union of the Comoros with a view to renewing the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the\r\nEuropean Community and the Union of the Comoros. At the end of the negotiations\r\na new draft protocol was initialled by the negotiators on 5 July 2013. The\r\nnew Protocol covers a period of three years from 1 January 2014, the date of\r\nprovisional application laid down in Article 13.\nThe main aim\r\nof the Protocol to the Agreement is to provide fishing opportunities for\r\nEuropean Union vessels in the fishing zone of the Union of the Comoros within the limits of the available surplus. The Commission\u2019s negotiating position\r\nwas based in part on the results of an ex-post evaluation carried out by\r\nexternal experts. \nThe general\r\naim is to strengthen cooperation between the European Union and the Union of\r\nthe Comoros, thereby creating a partnership framework within which to develop a\r\nsustainable fisheries policy and sound exploitation of fisheries resources in\r\nthe fishing zone of the Union of the Comoros, in the interests of both Parties.\r\n\nMore specifically, the\r\nProtocol provides for fishing opportunities in the following categories:\n\u2013     \r\n42 freezer tuna seiners; \n\u2013     \r\n20 surface longliners. \nThe method for allocating fishing\r\nopportunities among the Member States should be defined.\nThe Commission proposes, on this basis,\r\nthat the Council adopt the Regulation.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe interested parties were consulted\r\nduring the evaluation of the Protocol for the period 2011-13. Experts from the\r\nMember States were also consulted in technical meetings. These consultations\r\nled to the conclusion that it would be beneficial to maintain a fishing\r\nprotocol with the Union of the Comoros.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nThis procedure\r\nis being initiated in parallel with the procedures relating to the Council decision\r\non provisional application of the Protocol as well as the Council decision\r\nconcluding the Protocol as such. \n2013/0382 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\non the allocation of fishing opportunities\r\nunder the Protocol agreed between the European Union and the Union of the\r\nComoros setting out the fishing opportunities and financial contribution\r\nprovided for in the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the two parties\r\ncurrently in force\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 43(3)\r\nthereof, \nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 5 October 2006, the\r\nCouncil adopted Regulation (EC) No 1563/2006 on the conclusion of the Partnership\r\nAgreement in the fisheries sector between the European Community and the Union\r\nof the Comoros [2].\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The European Community and\r\nthe Union of the Comoros notified each other on 3 May 2007 and 6 March\r\n2008 of the completion of the procedures necessary for the entry into force of\r\nthe Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Union of the Comoros[3].\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A new Protocol to the\r\nPartnership Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the \u2018new Protocol\u2019) was\r\ninitialled on 5 July 2013. The new Protocol grants EU vessels fishing\r\nopportunities in the fishing zone over which the Union of the Comoros exercises its jurisdiction.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On [\u2026] the Council adopted\r\nDecision \u2026/2013/EU[4]\r\non the signing and provisional application of the new Protocol.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The method for allocating\r\nthe fishing opportunities among the Member States should be defined for the\r\nperiod of application of the new Protocol.\n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 10(1) of Council\r\nRegulation (EC) No 1006/2008[5]\r\nprovides that the Commission will inform the Member States concerned if it\r\nappears that the fishing opportunities allocated to the European Union under\r\nthe new Protocol are not fully exhausted.
