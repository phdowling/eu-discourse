concerning the adoption of a Decision amending the Convention of 20 May 1987 on a common transit procedure (adaptations resulting from the accession of Croatia to the Union) /* COM/2013/0467 final - 2013/0219 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nGrounds for and objectives of the\nproposal\nThe Convention of 20 May 1987 on a common\ntransit procedure (\"Convention\") establishes the measures\nfacilitating the movement of goods between the European Union and the Republic of Iceland, the Kingdom of Norway, the Swiss Confederation, the Republic of Croatia and the   Republic of Turkey.\nThe forthcoming accession of Croatia to the European Union on 1 July 2013 involves necessary technical adaptations to Appendix\nIII of the Convention, reflecting its new position as a Member State of the European Union.\nGeneral context\nThis proposal aims at reorganising the\nlinguistic references to Croatia in the relevant Annexes of Appendix III of the\nConvention and in particular by moving them from the EFTA countries part to the\nEuropean Union part. \nIn addition, in order to allow the use of\nthe guarantee forms printed in accordance with the criteria in force prior to\nthe date of accession of Croatia to the European Union, a transitional period\nshould be established during which the printed forms, with the corresponding\ngeographical adaptations, could continue to be used.\nExisting provisions in the area of the\nproposal\nThere are no existing provisions in the\narea of the proposal.\nConsistency with other policies and\nobjectives of the Union\nNot applicable.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS WITH THE\nINTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nConsultation of interested parties\nConsultation methods, main sectors\ntargeted and general profile of respondents\nConsultation with approval by the EU-EFTA\n\"Common transit\" working group, representing the Contracting Parties\nto the Convention.\nSummary of responses and how they\nhave been taken into account\nFavourable opinion.\nCollection and use of expertise\nThere was no need for external expertise.\nImpact assessment\nThere was no need for an impact assessment\nas the fundamental rules of the Convention continue to apply. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE PROPOSAL\nSummary of the proposed action\nThe Croatian language versions of the\nreferences used in the Convention should be reorganised and placed in the\nappropriate order. The guarantee forms should be adapted by moving Croatia into the respective order among the European Union Member States.\nConsequently, the provisions of the\nConvention on a common transit procedure have to be subject to the necessary adaptations.\nThis draft decision was communicated to the\nEU-EFTA Working Group on common transit.\nThe Council is invited to establish the EU\nposition regarding the annexed decision to be adopted by the EU-EFTA Joint\nCommittee on Common Transit. \nLegal basis\nArticle 15 of the Convention of 20 May 1987\non a common transit procedure. \nSubsidiarity principle\nThe proposal falls under the exclusive\ncompetence of the European Union. The subsidiarity principle therefore does not\napply. \nProportionality principle\nThe proposal complies with the\nproportionality principle for the following reason.\nNot applicable.\nChoice of instruments\nProposed instrument: Decision of the\nEU-EFTA Joint Committee.\nOther means would not be adequate for the\nfollowing reason:\nThere is no other adequate instrument.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 BUDGETARY IMPLICATION\nThe proposal has no implication for the EU\nbudget.\n5.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 OPTIONAL ELEMENTS\nSimplification\nThe common transit procedure provides for\nsimplification of administrative procedures for public authorities (EU or\nnational) and for private parties.\n2013/0219 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union, in the EU-EFTA Joint Committee concerning the adoption\nof a Decision amending the Convention of 20 May 1987 on a common transit\nprocedure (adaptations resulting from the accession of Croatia to the Union) \n\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\nof the European Union, and in particular Article 207(4), in conjunction with\nArticle 218(9), thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 1 of the\nConvention on a common transit procedure[1]\n(hereinafter \"the Convention\") stipulates that the Convention lays\ndown measures for the carriage of goods in transit between the Community and\nthe EFTA countries, as well as between the EFTA countries themselves, by\nintroducing a common transit procedure regardless of the kind and origin of the\ngoods.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 15 of the\nConvention empowers the Joint Committee set up by the Convention to recommend\nand adopt, by decisions, amendments to the Convention and its Appendices.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Croatia accedes to the Union on 1 July 2013. \n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Accordingly, the Croatian\nlanguage versions of the references used in the Convention should be moved from\nthe EFTA countries part and inserted in their respective order in the Union\nMember States part. \n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Technical amendments to\nthe Convention need also to be introduced as regards the guarantee forms listed\nin Appendix III, resulting from Croatia's accession to the Union.\n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The application of this\nDecision is linked to the date of accession of Croatia to the Union.\n(7)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In order to allow the use\nof guarantee forms printed in accordance with the criteria in force prior to\nthe date of accession of Croatia to the Union, a transitional period should be\nestablished during which the printed forms, with some adaptations, could\ncontinue to be used.\n(8)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Convention should be\namended accordingly.\n(9)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Therefore, the position of\nthe Union concerning the proposed amendment should be determined,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be taken by the Union in the EU-EFTA Joint Committee on common transit concerning the adoption of Decision\nNo XXX by the EU-EFTA Joint Committee amending the Convention of 20 May 1987 on\na common transit procedure shall be based on the draft Decision attached to\nthis Decision.\nMinor changes to the draft Decision may be\nagreed to by the representatives of the Union in the EU-EFTA Joint Committee.\nThe Council is to be duly informed about any such changes.\nArticle 2\nThe Commission shall publish the Decision\nof the EU-EFTA Joint Committee on common transit, once adopted, in the Official\nJournal of the European Union.\nArticle 3\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\nday following that of its adoption. \nDone at Brussels,
