Regulation (EU) No 472/2013\napplies to existing macroeconomic adjustment programmes that are in place as of\nits entry into force which, as a consequence, requires that adjustments follow\nthe Article 7(5) procedure.\n2013/0195 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL IMPLEMENTING DECISION\napproving the update of the macroeconomic\nadjustment programme of Portugal\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN
