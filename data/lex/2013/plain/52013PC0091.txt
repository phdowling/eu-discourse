concerning an amendment to Annex XXI to the EEA Agreement /* COM/2013/091 final - 2013/0054 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Annex XXI\n(Statistics) to the EEA Agreement by incorporating Commission Regulation (EU)\nNo 555/2012 of 22 June 2012 amending Regulation (EC) No 184/2005 of the\nEuropean Parliament and of the Council on Community statistics concerning\nbalance of payments, international trade in services and foreign direct investment,\nas regards the update of data requirements and definitions. \nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee provides for exemptions for Norway and Iceland not to report balance\nof payments data on a monthly basis to Eurostat as required in Table 1 of Annex\n1 in the Annex of Commission Regulation (EU) No 555/2012 of 22 June 2012\namending Regulation (EC) No 184/2005 of the European Parliament and of the\nCouncil since such data is either not produced in such frequency or would\nnecessitate the complete reorganisation of the data collection framework, while\nLiechtenstein is exempted from the application of this Regulation based on\npoint 19s of Annex XXI to the EEA Agreement \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf\non such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2013/0054 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union,\nin the EEA Joint Committee\nconcerning an amendment to Annex XXI\nto the EEA Agreement\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\nof the European Union, and in particular Article 338\n(1), in\nconjunction with Article 218 (9) Annex\nXXI thereto.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Annex XXI to the EEA\nAgreement contains specific provisions and arrangements concerning
