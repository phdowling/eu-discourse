The Council must decide on this proposal\r\nat the latest 21 calendar days following the presentation by the Commission of\r\nits proposal and Member States must pay the third instalment of the\r\ncontribution at the latest 21 calendar days following the date on which the\r\nCouncil decision is notified to them.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nIn accordance with Article 57(7) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the amount managed by the Commission and the amount\r\nmanaged by the EIB are specified for each.\nIn accordance with Article 145 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the EIB has sent the Commission its updated estimates\r\nof commitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\nArticle 58(2) of the 10th EDF\r\nFinancial Regulation provides that calls for contributions first use up the\r\namounts laid down for previous EDFs, one after the other. The calls for\r\ncontributions in this proposal therefore concern amounts under the 10th\r\nEDF for the Commission and under the 9th EDF for the EIB.\nArticle 60 of the 10th EDF Financial\r\nRegulation provides that a Member State which fails to pay the instalment of\r\nthe contribution due by the deadline will be required to pay interest on the\r\namount unpaid; the arrangements for the payment of interest are specified in\r\nthe same Article.\n2013/0335 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the financial contributions to be paid\r\nby Member States to
