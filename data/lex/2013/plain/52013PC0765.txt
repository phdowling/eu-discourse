"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0765\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION concerning the allocation of fishing opportunities under the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Seychelles /* COM/2013/0765 final - 2013/0373 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nOn the basis of relevant Council mandate[1], the Commission on behalf of\r\nthe European Union negotiated with the Republic of Seychelles in view of\r\nrenewing the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the\r\nEuropean Community and the Republic of Seychelles. Following these\r\nnegotiations, a new Protocol was initialled on the 10th of May 2013, which covers\r\na period of six years starting from the adoption of the Council decision on the\r\nProtocol's signing and provisional application and after expiry of the current\r\nProtocol, on 17 January 2014.\nThis procedure concerning the allocation\r\namong the Member States of the fishing opportunities under this Protocol is\r\nstarted in parallel with the procedures regarding the Council decision, with\r\nthe consent of the European Parliament, on the conclusion of the new Protocol,\r\nand the Council decision on the signing on behalf of the Union and the\r\nprovisional application of the said Protocol.\nThe new Protocol provides EU fishermen with\r\nfishing opportunities in Seychelles' fishing zone for 40 purse seiners and 6\r\nlong liners. In accordance with the Treaty, the method for allocating the\r\nfishing opportunities among the Member States should be defined.\nThe Commission proposes, on this basis, that\r\nthe Council adopt this Regulation.\n2013/0373 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\nconcerning the allocation of fishing\r\nopportunities under the Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between\r\nthe European Community and the Republic of Seychelles \nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 43, paragraph 3\r\nthereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nCommission[2],\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A new Protocol setting out the fishing opportunities and\r\nthe financial contribution provided for by the Fisheries Partnership Agreement\r\nbetween the European Community and the Republic of Seychelles was initialled on\r\n10 May 2013.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council has adopted on\r\n\u2026Decision No\u2026/2013/EU[3]\r\non the signing and provisional application of the new Protocol to the Fisheries\r\nPartnership Agreement.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The method for allocating\r\nthe fishing opportunities among the Member States should be defined for the\r\nperiod of application of the new Protocol.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with Article\r\n10(1) of Council Regulation (EC) No 1006/2008[4]\r\n, if it appears that the fishing opportunities allocated to the Union under the Protocol are not fully utilised, the Commission is to inform the Member\r\nStates concerned. The absence of a reply within a deadline to be set by the\r\nCouncil is to be considered as confirmation that the vessels of the Member\r\nStates concerned are not making full use of their fishing opportunities in the\r\ngiven period. It is necessary to set such deadline.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 This Regulation should\r\napply from the beginning of the provisional application of the Protocol, \nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The fishing opportunities set out in the Protocol to\r\nthe Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of   Seychelles ('the Protocol') shall be allocated among the Member States as\r\nfollows:\na) Tuna purse seiners\n Spain || Portugal || 2 vessels \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0  Regulation (EC) No\r\n1006/2008 shall apply without prejudice to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between\r\nthe European Community and the Republic of Seychelles ('the Agreement') and the\r\nProtocol.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 If applications for\r\nfishing States are to confirm that they do not fully utilise the fishing\r\nopportunities allocated under the Agreement, as referred to in Article 10(1) of\r\nRegulation (EC) No 1006/2008, shall be set at 10 working days from the day on which\r\nCommission informs the Member States that the fishing opportunities have not\r\nbeen exhausted.\nArticle 2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\r\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\r\nEuropean Union.\nIt shall apply from 18 January 2014.\nThis\r\nRegulation shall be binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all\r\nMember States.\nDone at Brussels,
