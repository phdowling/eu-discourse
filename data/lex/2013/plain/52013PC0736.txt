"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52013PC0736\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the financial contributions to be paid by Member States to finance the European Development Fund, including the ceiling for 2015, the amount for 2014 and the first instalment for 2014 /* COM/2013/0736 final - 2013/0352 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nThe Internal Agreement and Financial Regulation\r\nof the 10th EDF provide for a procedure for the calls for\r\ncontributions to be paid by Member States to finance the EDF. In accordance\r\nwith Article 57(2) of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation, this\r\nproposal covers:\n\u2013              \r\nthe ceiling for the annual amount of the\r\ncontribution for 2015;\n\u2013              \r\nthe annual amount of the contribution for 2014;\n\u2013              \r\nthe amount of the first instalment of the\r\ncontribution for 2014.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nIn accordance with Article 57(2) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the Council must decide on this proposal by 15\r\nNovember 2013 and Member States must pay the first instalment of the\r\ncontribution at the latest by 21 January 2014.\nIn accordance with Article 57(7) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the amount managed by the Commission and the amount\r\nmanaged by the EIB are specified for each.\nIn accordance with Article 58(2) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, calls for contributions should first use up the\r\namounts available from previous EDFs, one after the other. Taking into account that\r\nthe EIB doesn\u2019t call for contributions for the first instalment, this proposal\r\ntherefore concerns only amounts under the 10th EDF for the\r\nCommission.\nIn accordance with Article 145 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the EIB has sent the Commission its updated estimates\r\nof commitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\nIn accordance with Article 60 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, a Member State which fails to pay the instalment of\r\nthe contribution due by the deadline will be required to pay interest on the\r\namount unpaid; the arrangements for the payment of interest are specified in\r\nthe same Article.\n2013/0352 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the financial contributions to be paid\r\nby Member States to finance the European Development Fund, including the\r\nceiling for 2015, the amount for 2014 and the first instalment for 2014\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving financial assistance for the\r\nOverseas Countries and Territories to which Part Four of the EC Treaty applies,\r\nand in particular Article 7 thereof,\nHaving regard to the Council Regulation\r\n(EC) No 215/2008 of 18 February 2008 on the Financial Regulation applicable to\r\nthe 10th European Development Fund (hereinafter referred to as\r\n\"the 10th EDF Financial Regulation\"), and in particular\r\nArticle 57(5) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with the\r\nprocedure laid down in Articles 57 to 61 of the 10th EDF Financial\r\nRegulation, the Commission should present a proposal by 15 October 2013\r\nspecifying the ceiling for the annual amount of the Member States European\r\nDevelopment Fund (EDF) contribution for 2015, the amount of the contribution\r\nfor 2014 and the amount of the first instalment of the contribution for 2014.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with the\r\nfirst paragraph of Article 145 of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation,\r\nthe European Investment Bank has sent the Commission its updated estimates of\r\ncommitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with Article\r\n58(2) of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation, calls for contributions\r\nshould first use up the amounts available from previous EDFs, one after the\r\nother. Taking into account that the EIB doesn\u2019t call for contributions for the\r\nfirst instalment, this proposal therefore concerns only amounts under the 10th\r\nEDF for the Commission.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Council has adopted on\r\nthe 19th of November 2012, on a proposal made by the Commission, the\r\ndecision to fix at EUR 3.250.000.000 the Commission's share and at 360.000.000\r\nthe EIB's share of the ceiling of the Member States contributions to the EDF\r\nfor 2014,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe ceiling for the annual amount of the\r\nMember States EDF contributions for 2015 is hereby set at EUR 3,300,000,000\r\nfor the Commission, and at EUR 300,000,000 for the European Investment\r\nBank.\nArticle 2\nThe annual amount of the Member States EDF\r\ncontributions for 2014 is hereby set at EUR 3,100,000,000 for the\r\nCommission, and at EUR 150,000,000 for the European Investment Bank.\nArticle 3\nThe individual EDF contributions to be paid\r\nby the Member States to the Commission and the European Investment Bank as the first\r\ninstalment for 2014 are set out in the table in the Annex.\nArticle 4\nThis Decision is addressed to the Member\r\nStates.\nDone at
