\n   ||   \nAnnex\nRULES\r\nFOR THE ADMINISTRATION AND IMPLEMENTATION OF THE TARIFF RATE QUOTAS \nArticle 1\r\nTiming of auctions and license application\n(1)                   \r\nAuctions for skim milk powder, whole milk powder,\r\ncondensed milk and natural honey shall precede the import period, i.e. in June\r\nfor quotas opening in July.\n(2)                   \r\nThe specific guidelines by the Korea\r\nAgro-Fisheries & Food Trade Corporation will provide that the public\r\nnotices for the auctions will be made eight calendar days prior to the deadline\r\nof the application for participating in the auctions. \n(3)                   \r\nFor product items whose timeline of quota\r\nlicense application is not fixed in the Korea-EU Free Trade Agreement, the\r\nspecific guidelines of the agencies designated to manage Tariff Rate Quotas\r\n(TRQs) (hereinafter referred to as \u201cRecommending Agencies\u201d) will provide for a\r\nlicense application period of at least seven calendar days from the first\r\nworking day of the execution year referred to in Annex 2-A, point 5 of the\r\nKorea-EU Free Trade Agreement. \nArticle 2\r\nDeposit and fees\n(1)                   \r\nThere will be no administrative cost or fees\r\ncharged other than the deposit \u2013 the amount paid by bidders applying for\r\nauction - for services related to an application for a TRQ allocation through\r\nauction.\n(2)                   \r\nThe general instructions to bidders for each\r\nauction will prescribe that the deposit is to be returned to bidders as soon as\r\nallowed by normal administration procedures after the auction is completed.\n(3)                   \r\nNo guarantee or deposit will be required for\r\nlicense applicants.\nArticle 3\r\nIssuance and validity of a recommendation\n(1)                   \r\nRecommending Agencies will issue a Korea-EU FTA\r\nTRQs Import Recommendation (hereinafter referred to as \u201cRecommendation\u201d)\r\nfollowing an auction or a licence application.\n(2)                   \r\nRecommendations to the applicants shall be\r\nissued within two calendar days provided the submitted TRQs Recommendation\r\napplication meets the Recommendation requirements. \n(3)                   \r\nA Recommendation for TRQs import will be valid\r\nfor 90 days. The validity period may be extended for another 30 days, but\r\ncannot exceed the last day of the execution year referred to in Annex 2-A,\r\npoint 5 of the Korea-EU Free Trade Agreement.\nArticle 4\r\nPublishing information on TRQ administration\n(1)                   \r\nThe guidelines of the Ministry of Agriculture, Food\r\nand Rural Affairs of Korea (hereinafter referred to as \"MAFRA\") and\r\nspecific guidelines of Recommending Agencies will be published on their\r\nrespective websites. \n(2)                   \r\nThe Recommending Agencies will publish on a\r\nregular basis on their websites information that includes public notice for\r\nlicence applications and auctions, license allocation and auction periods,\r\nallocated quantities, remaining quantities available for each TRQ and the\r\nplanned date of the next auction/licensing period.\n(3)                   \r\nThe basic criteria for auctions including\r\neligibility, payment and return of deposit, auction dates, auction code and\r\ninformation will be contained in the specific guidelines by the Korea\r\nAgro-Fisheries & Food Trade Corporation or in the general instructions to\r\nbidders for each auction.\nArticle 5\r\nTRQ allocation rules\nThe MAFRA guidelines allow the distribution\r\nof the annual TRQ volume throughout the year by dividing the annual TRQ volume\r\ninto several sub-volumes, the sum of which equals the annual TRQ volume.
