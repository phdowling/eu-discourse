"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52014JC0037\n\nJoint Proposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION amending Regulation (EU) No 36/2012 concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Syria /* JOIN/2014/037 final - 2014/0323 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)                   \r\nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 36/2012 of 18 January\r\n2012 concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Syria gave effect to Council Decision 2011/782/CFSP[1].\r\nCouncil Decision 2012/739/CFSP[2]\r\nrepealed and replaced Decision 2011/782/CFSP. On 1 June 2013 Decision\r\n2012/739/CFSP expired. It was succeeded by Decision 2013/255/CFSP which applies\r\nuntil 1 June 2014. \n(2)                   \r\nCouncil Decision 2014/XXX/CFSP of XXX amended\r\nCouncil Decision 2013/255/CFSP to provide for further restrictive measures with\r\nrespect to Syria, in particular on jet fuel and certain fuel additives.\n(3)                   \r\nFurther action by the Union is needed in order\r\nto implement these measures. The High Representative of the Union for Foreign\r\nAffairs and Security Policy and the Europan Commission propose to amend\r\nRegulation (EU) No 36/2012 accordingly.\n2014/0323 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Regulation (EU) No 36/2012\r\nconcerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Syria\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\r\nof the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof, \nHaving regard to Council Decision 2013/255/CFSP\r\nof 31 May 2013 concerning restrictive measures against Syria[3],\r\n\nHaving regard to the joint proposal from\r\nthe High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy\r\nand the European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\r\n36/2012[4]\r\ngives effect to most of the measures provided for in Decision 2013/255/CFSP.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On \u2026November 2014 the\r\nCouncil adopted Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP amending Decision 2013/255/CFSP to prevent\r\njet fuels and additives being sold, supplied, transferred or exported, whether\r\nor not originating in the Union, to any person, entity or body in Syria, or for\r\nuse in Syria.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Furthermore, it should be\r\nprohibited to provide financing or financial assistance, including financial\r\nderivates, as well as insurance and reinsurance, to any person, entity or body\r\nin Syria or for use in Syria with respect to the sale, supply, transfer or\r\nexport of jet fuels and additives in Syria, or for use in Syria.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is necessary to provide\r\nfor a catch-all anti-circumvention clause, providing that it shall be\r\nprohibited to participate, knowling and intentionally, in activities the object\r\nor effect of which is to circumvent the provisions of this Regulation. \n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 These measure fall within\r\nthe scope of the Treaty and, therefore, in particular with a view to ensuring\r\nits uniform application by economic operators in all Member States, action at\r\nthe level of the Union is necessary in order to implement them. \n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Regulation (EU) No 36/2012\r\nshould therefore be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nRegulation (EU) No 36/2012 is amended as\r\nfollows:\n\u00a0(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The following Article 8a is\r\ninserted: \n\u201cArticle\r\n8a\n1. It shall be prohibited:\n(a) to sell, supply, transfer or export,\r\ndirectly or indirectly, jet fuel and fuel additives as identified in Annex VIII\r\nto any person, entity or body in Syria, or for use in Syria; \n(b) to provide financing or financial\r\nassistance, including financial derivatives, as well as insurance and\r\nreinsurance related to the sale, supply, transfer or export of jet fuel and fuel\r\nadditives as identified in Annex VIII to any person, entity or body in Syria,\r\nor for use in Syria;\n(c) to provide brokering services with regard\r\nto the sale, supply, transfer or export of jet fuel and fuel additives as\r\nidentified in Annex VIII to any person, entity or body in Syria, or for use in   Syria. \n2. Annex VIII shall include jet fuel and fuel additives\r\nspecifically formulated for kerosene jet fuel.\n3. By way of derogation from paragraph 1, the\r\ncompetent authorities in the Member States as identified on the websites listed\r\nin Annex III may authorise, the sale, supply, transfer or export of jet fuel\r\nand fuel additives as identified in Annex VIII to any person, entity or body in\r\nSyria, or for use in Syria under such conditions as they deem appropriate,\r\nhaving determined that the jet fuel and fuel additives are:\n(a) necessary for delivery or facilitating the\r\ndelivery of assistance in Syria by the United Nations, or bodies acting on its\r\nbehalf, for humanitarian purposes such as delivering or facilitating the\r\ndelivery of assistance, including medical supplies, food or the transfer of\r\nhumanitarian workers and related assistance, or for evacuations from Syria or within\r\nSyria;\n(b) exclusively for the purposes of the\r\noperation of civilian aircraft operating within Syria. \n4. The Member States concerned shall inform the\r\nother Member States and the Commission within four weeks of authorisations\r\ngranted under this article.\n5. The prohibition in paragraph 1 shall not\r\napply to jet fuel and fuel additives as listed in Annex VIII exclusively used by\r\ncivilian aircraft flying from or into airports within Syria or transiting through Syria.\u201d\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 27a is inserted: \n\u201cArticle\r\n27a\n1. It shall be prohibited to participate,\r\nknowingly and intentionally, in activities the object or effect of which is to\r\ncircumvent the provisions of this Regulation.\u201d\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Annex to this Regulation is\r\ninserted as Annex VIII of Regulation (EU) No 36/2012.\nArticle 2\nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\r\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\r\nEuropean Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\r\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
