"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52014PC0631\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the financial contributions to be paid by Member states to finance the European Development fund in 2014, including the third instalment for 2014 /* COM/2014/0631 final - 2014/0292 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nThe Internal Agreement and Financial\r\nRegulation of the 10th EDF provide for a procedure for the calls for\r\ncontributions to be paid by Member States to finance the EDF. In accordance\r\nwith Article 57(3) of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation, this\r\nproposal covers:\n\u2013     \r\nthe amount of the third instalment of the\r\ncontribution for 2014.\nIn accordance with Article 57(7) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the amount managed by the Commission and the amount\r\nmanaged by the EIB are specified separately.\nIn accordance with Article 145 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the EIB has sent the Commission its updated estimates\r\nof commitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\nIn accordance with Article 58(2) of the\r\nFinancial Regulation calls for contributions should first use up the amounts\r\navailable from previous EDFs, one after the other. The calls for contributions\r\nin this proposal therefore concern amounts under the 10th EDF for the\r\nCommission.\nIn accordance with Article 57(5) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the Council must decide on this proposal at the\r\nlatest 21 calendar days following the presentation by the Commission of its\r\nproposal and Member States must pay the third instalment at the latest 21\r\ncalendar days following the date on which the Council decision was notified to\r\nthem.\nArticle 60(1) of the Financial Regulation\r\nprovides that a Member State which fails to pay the instalment of the\r\ncontribution due by the deadline will be required to pay interest on the amount\r\nunpaid; the arrangements for the payment of interest are specified in the same\r\nArticle.\n2014/0292 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the financial contributions to be paid\r\nby Member states to finance the European Development fund in 2014, including\r\nthe third instalment for 2014\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union,\nHaving regard to the Internal Agreement\r\nbetween the Representatives of the Governments of the Member States, meeting\r\nwithin the Council, on the financing of Community aid under the multiannual\r\nfinancial framework for the period 2008 to 2013 in accordance with the ACP-EC\r\nPartnership Agreement and on the allocation of financial assistance for the\r\nOverseas Countries and Territories to which Part Four of the EC Treaty applies[1], (\"the Internal\r\nAgreement\") and in particular Article 7 thereof,\nHaving regard to the Council Regulation\r\n(EC) No 215/2008 of 18 February 2008 on the Financial Regulation applicable to\r\nthe 10th European Development Fund (hereinafter referred to as\r\n\"the 10th EDF Financial Regulation\")[2] as last amended on 11\r\nApril 2011[3],\r\nand in particular Article 57(5) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with the\r\nprocedure laid down in Articles 57 to 61 of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation,\r\nthe Commission is presenting a proposal by 10 October specifying (a)\r\nthe amount of the third instalment of the contribution for 2014, and (b) a\r\nrevised annual amount of the contribution for the year 2014 in line with actual\r\nneeds, in case where, in accordance with Article 7(3) of the Internal\r\nAgreement, the annual amount deviate from actual needs. \n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Council has adopted on\r\nthe 8th of November 2013, on a proposal made by the Commission, the decision to\r\nfix at EUR 3.250.000.000 the total annual amount of the Member States\r\ncontributions to the EDF for 2014. The initial repartition was set at EUR\r\n3.100.000.000 for the Commission's share and at 150.000.000 for the EIB's share.\r\n\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with the\r\nfirst paragraph of Article 145 of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation, the\r\nEuropean Investment Bank has sent the Commission its updated estimates of\r\ncommitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Following the request of\r\nboth the Commission and the EIB, the total amount for 2014 respects the ceiling\r\nof EUR 3.250.000.000 with a new repartition of the different share of the\r\nMember States contributions to the EDF: (i) EUR 3.144.308.777,01 for the\r\nCommission's share and (ii) EUR 105.691.222,99 for the EIB's share.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 58(2) of the 10th\r\nEuropean Development Fund (hereafter \"the EDF\") Financial Regulation\r\nprovides that calls for contributions first use up the amounts provided for\r\nprevious EDFs.\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe individual EDF contributions to be paid\r\nby the Member States to the Commission as the third instalment for 2014 are set\r\nout in the table in the Annex.\nArticle 2\nThis decision shall apply from the date of\r\nits adoption.\nArticle 3\nThis Decision is addressed to the Member\r\nStates.\nDone at
