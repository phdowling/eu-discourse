November 2014. \nRenewal of the Agreement for five more\r\nyears would be in the interest of both Parties so as to maintain continuity in\r\nthe scientific and technological relations between Ukraine and the European\r\nUnion.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nAt the meeting of the Joint Community-Ukraine\r\nCommittee, set up under Article 6 of the Agreement, held in Kiev on 24 May\r\n2013, both Parties agreed that the Agreement should be renewed for a further\r\nperiod of five years.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nThe material content of the renewed\r\nAgreement will be identical to that of the current
