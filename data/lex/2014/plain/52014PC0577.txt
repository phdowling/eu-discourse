2014/0266 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\r\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\r\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\r\npossible after its adoption. \nArticle 78 of the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Contracting Parties shall strengthen and broaden cooperation in the\r\nframework of the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, environment,\r\nand\u00a0 research and technological development. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31\r\nto the EEA Agreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four\r\nfreedoms in order to extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties in the\r\narea of environment, and research and technological development.\nIn order to extend the cooperation of the\r\nContracting Parties to the EEA Agreement in this area (to take place from 1\r\nJanuary 2014), Regulation (EU) No 377/2014 of the European Parliament and of\r\nthe Council of 3 April 2014 establishing the Copernicus Programme and repealing\r\nRegulation (EU) No 911/2010[1]\r\nneeds to be incorporated into the EEA Agreement through the above mentioned\r\namendment.\nIt should be noted that Norway and Liechtenstein decided not to participate in this programme. Norway is considering\r\nstarting its participation as of 2015. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\r\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\r\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\r\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\r\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\nThe Decision of the EEA Joint Committee\r\nshould serve also as the agreement of the Parties, pursuant to Article 1(8) of\r\nProtocol 32 to the EEA Agreement, to commence the cooperation under Regulation\r\n(EU) No 377/2014 from 1 January 2014, even if this EEA Joint Committee Decision\r\nis adopted, or if the fulfilment of constitutional requirements for this EEA\r\nJoint Committee Decision, if any, is notified after 10 July 2014.\n2014/0266 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position
