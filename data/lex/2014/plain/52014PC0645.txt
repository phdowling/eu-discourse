In accordance with Article 57(3) of\r\nthe 10th EDF Financial Regulation, this proposal covers:\n\u2013     \r\nThe ceiling of the contributions from Member\r\nStates for 2016;\n\u2013     \r\nThe amount of the contributions for 2015; \n\u2013     \r\nThe amount of the first instalment of the\r\ncontribution for 2015.\nIn accordance with Article 57(7) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the amount managed by the Commission and the amount\r\nmanaged by the EIB are specified separately.\nIn accordance with Article 145 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the EIB has sent the Commission its updated estimates\r\nof commitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\nIn accordance with Article 58(2) of the\r\nFinancial Regulation calls for contributions should first use up the amounts\r\navailable from previous EDFs, one after the other. The calls for contributions\r\nin this proposal therefore concern amounts under the 10th EDF for both the EIB\r\n(1st call under the 10th EDF) and the Commission.\nIn accordance with Article 57(3) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the Council must decide on this proposal at the\r\nlatest by the 15th of November 2014, and Member States must pay the first\r\ninstalment at the latest by the 21st January 2015.\nArticle 60(1) of the 10th EDF Financial\r\nRegulation provides that a Member State which fails to pay the instalment of\r\nthe contribution due by the deadline will be required to pay interest on the\r\namount unpaid; the arrangements for the payment of interest are specified in\r\nthe same Article.\n2014/0298 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the financial contributions to be paid\r\nby Member states to finance the European Development fund, including the\r\nceiling for 2016, the amount for 2015 and the first instalment for 2015.\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE
