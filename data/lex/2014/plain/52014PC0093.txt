Negotiations with Norway were successfully concluded by the initialling of the Additional Protocol.\nThe proposed Additional\r\nProtocol makes the Republic of Croatia a contracting party to the Agreement and\r\ncommits the EU to providing an authentic version of the Agreement in the new\r\nofficial language of the EU. It also provides for additional fish trade\r\nconcessions for Norway, in view of Croatia's accession to the European Economic\r\nArea.\nThe Additional Protocol was signed in Brussels on (...). \nThe
