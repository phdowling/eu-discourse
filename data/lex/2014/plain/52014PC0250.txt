concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0250 final - 2014/0133 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\r\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\r\nintegrate all the relevant Union legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\r\npossible after its adoption and also allow for the participation of the EEA\r\nEFTA States in EEA relevant EU actions, activities or programmes.\nArticle 78 of the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Contracting Parties shall strengthen and broaden cooperation in the framework\r\nof the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, small and medium-sized\r\nenterprises. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31\r\nto the EEA Agreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four\r\nfreedoms in order to extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties in the\r\narea of small and medium-sized enterprises.\nIt is therefore needed to incorporate in\r\nthe EEA Agreement Regulation (EU) No 1287/2013 of the European Parliament and\r\nof the Council of 11 December 2013 establishing a Programme for the\r\nCompetitiveness of Enterprises and small and medium-sized enterprises (COSME)\r\n(2014 - 2020) and repealing Decision No 1639/2006/EC[1].\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement should\r\ntherefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to take\r\nplace from 1 January 2014.\nIt should be noted that Liechtenstein and Norway shall be exempted from the participation in, and the financial contribution\r\nto, this programme.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\r\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\r\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\r\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\r\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 BUDGETARY IMPLICATION \nIt is proposed that Protocol 31 to the Agreement\r\non the EEA be amended in order to allow EEA/EFTA States to participate in the\r\nprogramme and to contribute financially to it in accordance with the conditions\r\nlaid down in the EEA Agreement.\n2014/0133
