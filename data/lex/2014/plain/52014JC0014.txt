2014/0113 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.     \nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 1284/2009[1] of 22 December 2009 imposed\ncertain restrictive measures in respect of the Republic of Guinea, in accordance with Common Position 2009/788/CFSP[2]\n(replaced by Council Decision 2010/638/CFSP[3]).\nThese measures include the prohibition on the provision of technical and\nfinancial assistance and other services related to military equipment and the\nembargo on the equipment which might be used for internal repression.\n2.     \nThe Council is preparing a decision amending\nCouncil Decision 2010/638/CFSP in order to lift the arms embargo and the\nembargo on the equipment which might be used for internal repression.\n3.     \nAction at EU level is required to give effect to\nthe lifting of these measures.\n4.     \nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 1284/2009 should therefore\nbe amended.\n2014/0113 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Council Regulation (EU) No\n1284/2009 imposing certain specific restrictive measures in respect of the Republic of   Guinea\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215(1) thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Decision\n2010/638/CFSP concerning restrictive measures against the Republic of Guinea[4],\nHaving regard to the joint proposal from\nthe High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy\nand the Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\n1284/2009[5]\nimposed certain restrictive measures in respect of the Republic of Guinea, in accordance with Council Common Position 2009/788/CFSP[6]\n(replaced by Council Decision 2010/638/CFSP). These measures include the\nprohibition on the provision of technical and financial assistance and other\nservices related to military equipment and the embargo on the equipment which\nmight be used for internal repression.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On \u2026 March 2014 the\nCouncil adopted Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP amending Council Decision 2010/638/CFSP lifting\nthe arms embargo and the embargo on the equipment which might be used for\ninternal repression.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Certain aspects of the\nlifting of these measures fall within the scope of the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union and, therefore, notably with a view to\nensuring their uniform application by economic operators in all Member States, regulatory\naction at the level of the Union is necessary in order to implement it.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\n1284/2009 should be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nRegulation (EU) No 1284/2009 is amended as\nfollows:\n(1)                   \nIn Article 1, points (a), (b) and (c) are\ndeleted.\n(2)                   \nArticles 2, 3, 4 and 5 are deleted.\n(3)                   \nAnnex I is deleted.\n(4)                   \nAnnex III is replaced by the text set out in the\nAnnex to this Regulation.\nArticle 2 \nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\nEuropean Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at Brussels,
