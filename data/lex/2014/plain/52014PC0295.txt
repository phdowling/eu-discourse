SUB-COMMITTEE ON SANITARY\r\nAND PHYTOSANITARY MEASURES ESTABLISHED IN CHAPTER 5 OF THE TRADE AGREEMENT\r\nBETWEEN THE EUROPEAN UNION AND ITS MEMBER STATES, OF THE ONE PART AND COLOMBIA\r\nAND PERU, OF THE OTHER PART\nArticle 1\r\nComposition and Chair\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Sub-committee on Sanitary and\r\nPhytosanitary Measures (hereinafter referred to as the \u201cSPS Sub-committee\u201d)\r\nthat is established in accordance with Article 103 of the Trade Agreement\r\nbetween the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and Colombia\r\nand Peru, of the other part shall perform its duties as provided in Article 103\r\nof the Trade Agreement and take responsibility to ensure and monitor the\r\nimplementation of Chapter 5 on Sanitary and Phytosanitary measures.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 As provided for in Article 103.2\r\nof the Trade Agreement, the SPS Sub-committee shall be composed of the\r\nrepresentatives designated by each Party with responsibilities for sanitary and\r\nphytosanitary matters.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The SPS-Sub-committee shall be\r\nchaired, on a rotational basis, for a period of one year by a senior official\r\nof the European Commission, on behalf of the Union and its Member States, by a senior official of the Colombian Government, or by a senior official of the\r\nPeruvian Government. The first chairing period shall begin on the date of the\r\nfirst Trade Committee meeting and end on 31 December of the same year. The SPS Sub-committee\r\nshall be chaired by the Party holding the chair in the Trade Committee.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The SPS Sub-committee may meet in\r\nsessions where only the EU Party and one signatory Andean Country participate,\r\nregarding matters which relate exclusively to their bilateral relationship.\r\nSuch sessions will be co-chaired by both Parties. Other signatory Andean\r\nCountries may participate in such sessions, subject to prior agreement of the Parties\r\nconcerned. \n5.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Reference to the Parties in this\r\nRules of Procedure is in accordance with the definition provided for in Article\r\n6 of the Agreement.\nArticle 2\r\nMeetings\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The SPS Sub-committee shall meet\r\nat least once a year and will hold special sessions upon request of any Party,\r\nas provided for in Article 103.2 of the Agreement. The meetings shall be held\r\non a rotational basis, in Bogota, Brussels and Lima, unless the Parties agree\r\notherwise.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The meeting of the SPS Sub-committee\r\nshall be convened by the Party holding the Chair at a date and venue agreed by\r\nthe Parties.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The SPS Sub-committee may also\r\nmeet by video and audio conference.\nArticle 3\r\nDelegations\nBefore each meeting, the\r\nParties shall be informed of the intended composition of the delegations\r\nattending the meeting.\nArticle 4\r\nObservers\nThe SPS Sub-committee may\r\ndecide to invite observers on an ad hoc basis.\nArticle 5\r\nCorrespondence\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The documents of the SPS\r\nSub-committee provided for in Articles 6, 7 and 8 of these Rules of Procedure\r\nshall be forwarded to the chairs of the SPS Sub-committee and to the\r\nSecretariat of the Trade Committee. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 For matters which relate\r\nexclusively to a bilateral relationship between the EU and one signatory Andean\r\nCountry, the correspondence will be done between these two Parties, keeping the\r\nother signatory Andean Countries fully informed, as appropriate.\nArticle 6\r\nAgenda for the Meetings\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 A provisional agenda for each\r\nmeeting shall be drawn up by the Parties jointly. It shall be forwarded,\r\ntogether with the relevant documents, to all the Parties no later than 14 days\r\nbefore the beginning of the meeting.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The agenda shall be adopted by\r\nthe SPS Sub-committee at the beginning of each meeting. Items other than those\r\nappearing on the provisional agenda may be placed on the agenda if the Parties\r\nso agree. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Chairperson of the SPS\r\nSub-committee may, upon agreement of the other Parties, invite experts to\r\nattend its meetings in order to provide information on specific subjects. \nArticle 7\r\nMinutes\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Draft minutes of each meeting\r\nshall be drawn up jointly by the Parties. The first draft will be prepared by\r\nthe Party acting as Chair within 21 days from the end of the meeting.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The minutes shall, as a general\r\nrule, summarise each item on the agenda, specifying, where applicable: \n(a)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the documents submitted to the SPS\r\nSub-committee;\n(b)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 any statement that a member of the SPS\r\nSub-committee has asked to be entered; and \n(c)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the decisions proposed,\r\nrecommendations made, statements agreed upon and conclusions adopted on\r\nspecific items. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The minutes shall also include a\r\nlist of participants in the SPS Sub-committee meeting.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The minutes shall be approved in\r\nwriting by the Parties within two months of the date of the meeting. Once\r\napproved, copies shall be signed by the Chairperson and his/her counterparts of\r\nthe other Parties. Each of the Parties shall receive one original copy of these\r\nauthentic documents. A copy of the signed minutes shall be forwarded to the\r\nSecretariat of the Trade Committee.\nArticle 8\r\nAction Plan\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The SPS Sub-committee shall adopt\r\nan action plan reflecting the actions agreed upon the Parties during the\r\nmeeting.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The implementation by the Parties\r\nof the action plan will be reviewed by the SPS Sub-committee in its next\r\nmeeting.\nArticle 9\r\nLanguages\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The official languages of the SPS\r\nSub-committee shall be the official languages of the Parties.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Unless otherwise decided, the SPS\r\nSub-committee shall normally base its deliberations on documentation and\r\nproposals prepared in the languages referred to in paragraph 1.\nArticle 10\r\nPublicity and Confidentiality\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Unless otherwise decided, the\r\nmeetings of the SPS Sub-committee shall not be public. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 When a Party submits information\r\nconsidered as confidential under its laws and regulations to the SPS\r\nSub-committee, specialised committees, working groups or any other bodies, the\r\nParties shall treat that information as confidential, according to the rules\r\ndescribed in Article 290.2 of the Agreement.\nArticle 11\r\nExpenses\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Each Party shall bear any\r\nexpenses it incurs as a result of participating in the meetings of the SPS\r\nSub-committee, both with regard to staff, travel and subsistence expenses and\r\nwith regard to postal and telecommunications expenses. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Expenses in connection with the\r\norganisation of meetings and reproduction of documents shall be borne by the\r\nParty hosting the meeting. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Expenses in connection with the\r\ninterpretation at meetings and translation of documents into or from Spanish\r\nand English shall be borne by the Party hosting the meeting. Interpretation and\r\ntranslation into or from the other languages shall be borne by the requesting\r\nParty. \nArticle 12\r\nAmendment of Rules of Procedure\nThese\r\nRules of Procedure, as well as any subsequent amendment, are to be adopted by\r\nthe SPS Sub-committee as foreseen by Article 103.2 of the Agreement.\n\n\n\n\nTop  \n  "
