The text of the Joint Committee Decision has been drawn up in cooperation\nwith the Swiss authorities. No impact assessment has been carried out. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ASPECTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nThe Decision is based on the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 207(4) in\nconjunction with Article 218(9) thereof.\n2014/0015 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be taken by the\nEuropean Union within the Joint Committee on Agriculture set up by the\nAgreement between the European Community and the Swiss Confederation on trade\nin agricultural products, as regards the amendment of Decision No 2/2003\nof that Committee\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 207(4) in\nconjunction with Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Agreement between the\nEuropean Community and the Swiss Confederation on trade in agricultural\nproducts[1]\n(hereinafter referred to as \u2018the Agreement\u2019) entered into force on 1 June 2002.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Article 6 of the\nAgreement sets up a Joint Committee on Agriculture (hereinafter referred to\nas \u2018the Committee\u2019), which is responsible for the administration of\nthe Agreement and ensures its good functioning.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In accordance with Article\n6(4) and (7) of the Agreement, on 21 October 2003 the Committee\nadopted its Rules of Procedure[2]\nand set up the working groups needed to administer the Annexes to the Agreement[3].\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 By Decision No 2/2003\nof the Joint Committee several working groups, including the \u2018PDO/PGI\u2019 working\ngroup, were set up and their terms of reference adopted.
