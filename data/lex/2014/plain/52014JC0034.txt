"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52014JC0034\n\nJoint Proposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION repealing Regulation (EC) No 2488/2000 maintaining a freeze of funds in relation to Mr Milosevic and those persons associated with him /* JOIN/2014/034 final - 2014/0271 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)              \r\nPursuant to Council Regulation (EC) No 2488/2000[1] all funds and other\r\nfinancial resources held outside the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia belonging\r\nto Mr Milosevic and to natural persons associated with him, are to be frozen\r\nand such funds and financial resources are not to be made available to or for\r\nthe benefit of those persons, as listed in Annex I to the Regulation. \n(2)              \r\nBy means of Council Decision [\u2026] the Council\r\nrepealed its Common Position 2000/696/CFSP. The Council decided that there is\r\nno ground to continue to apply these restrictive measures, as the persons\r\nlisted in the Annex no longer continue to represent a threat to the\r\nconsolidation of democracy.\n(3)              \r\nThe High Representative of the EU for Foreign\r\nAffairs and Security Policy and the Commission propose to repeal Council\r\nRegulation (EC) No 2488/2000 with immediate effect.\n2014/0271 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\nrepealing Regulation (EC) No 2488/2000\r\nmaintaining a freeze of funds in relation to\r\nMr Milosevic and those persons associated with him \nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Decision [\u2026][2] repealing Common Position 2000/1696/CFSP on the maintenance of specific\r\nrestrictive measures directed against Mr Milosevic and persons associated with\r\nhim,\nHaving regard to the joint proposal of the\r\nHigh Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and of\r\nthe Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)              \r\nPursuant to Council Regulation (EC) No 2488/2000[3] all funds and other\r\nfinancial resources held outside the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia belonging\r\nto Mr Milosevic and to natural persons associated with him, are to be frozen\r\nand such funds and financial resources are not to be made available to or for\r\nthe benefit of those persons, as listed in Annex I to the Regulation. \n(2)              \r\nBy means of Council Decision [\u2026][4] the Council repealed\r\nits Common Position 2000/696/CFSP[5].\r\nThe Council decided that there is no ground to continue to apply these restrictive\r\nmeasures, as the persons listed in the Annex no longer continue to represent a\r\nthreat to the consolidation of democracy.\n(3)              \r\nIt is therefore appropriate to repeal Regulation\r\n(EC) No 2488/2000 with immediate effect,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nRegulation (EC) No 2488/2000 is hereby repealed.\nArticle 2 \nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\r\nthe day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\r\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
