concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0218 final - 2014/0122 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption. \nArticle 78 of the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Contracting Parties shall strengthen and broaden cooperation in the\nframework of the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, civil\nprotection. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31\nto the EEA Agreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four\nfreedoms in order to extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties in the\narea of civil protection.\nIn order to extend the cooperation of the\nContracting Parties to the EEA Agreement in this field, Decision No\n1313/2013/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of 17 December 2013\non a Union Civil Protection Mechanism[1]\nneeds to be incorporated in the EEA Agreement.\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement should\ntherefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to take\nplace from 1 January 2014.\nIt should be noted that Liechtenstein shall be exempted from the participation in, and the financial contribution\nto, this programme.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2014/0122 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union,\nin the EEA Joint Committee concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in on the\nEuropean Economic Area[3]\n(\u201cthe EEA Agreement\u201d) entered into force on 1 January 1994.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 98 of\nthe EEA Agreement, the EEA
