JOIN/2014/042 final - 2014/0357 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n(1)              \r\nCouncil Regulation (EU) No 267/2012 of 23 March\r\n2012 concerning restrictive measures against Iran gives effect to the measures\r\nprovided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP.\n(2)              \r\nOn \u2026 December 2014, the Council adopted Decision\r\n2014/\u2026/CFSP extending until 30 June 2015 the exemption provided for in paragraph\r\n14 of Article 20 concerning acts and transactions carried out with regard to listed\r\nentities insofar as necessary for the execution of the obligations provided for\r\nin contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 or in ancillary contracts\r\nnecessary for the execution of such obligations where the supply of Iranian\r\ncrude oil and petroleum products or the proceeds derived from their supply are\r\nfor the reimbursement of outstanding amounts with respect to contracts\r\nconcluded before 23 January 2012 to persons or entities within the territories\r\nof Member States or under their jurisdiction, where those contracts\r\nspecifically provide for such reimbursements. \n(3)              \r\nFurther action by the Union is needed in order\r\nto implement this extension.\n(4)              \r\nThe High Representative of the Union for Foreign\r\nAffairs and Security Policy and the European Commission propose to amend\r\nRegulation (EU) No 267/2012 accordingly.\n2014/0357 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\namending Regulation (EU) No 267/2012\r\nconcerning restrictive measures against Iran\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof, \nHaving regard to Council Decision 2010/413/CFSP\r\nof 26 July 2010 concerning restrictive measures against Iran and repealing Common Position 2007/140/CFSP [1],\nHaving regard to the joint proposal of the\r\nHigh Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and of\r\nthe European Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Council Regulation (EU) No\r\n267/2012[2]\r\ngives effect to the measures provided for in Decision 2010/413/CFSP.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On \u2026 December 2014, the\r\nCouncil adopted Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP amending Decision\r\n2010/413/CFSP to extend until 30 June 2015 the exemption\r\nprovided for in paragraph 14 of Article 20 concerning acts and transactions\r\ncarried out with regard to listed entities insofar as necessary for the\r\nexecution of the obligations provided for in contracts concluded before 23\r\nJanuary 2012 or in ancillary contracts necessary for the execution of such\r\nobligations where the supply of Iranian crude oil and petroleum products or the\r\nproceeds derived from their supply are for the reimbursement of outstanding\r\namounts with respect to contracts concluded before 23 January 2012 to persons\r\nor entities within the territories of Member States or under their\r\njurisdiction, where those contracts specifically provide for such\r\nreimbursements.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 This measure falls within\r\nthe scope of the Treaty and regulatory action at the level of the Union is therefore necessary in order to implement it, in particular with a view to\r\nensuring its uniform application by economic operators in all Member States.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Regulation (EU) No\r\n267/2012 should therefore be amended accordingly,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nIn point (b) of Article 28a of Regulation\r\n(EU) No 267/2012, the words \u2018until 31 December 2014\u2019 are replaced by the words\r\n\u2018until 30 June 2015\u2019.\nArticle 2 \nThis Regulation shall enter into force on\r\nthe day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the\r\nEuropean Union.\nThis Regulation shall be binding\r\nin its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.\nDone at
