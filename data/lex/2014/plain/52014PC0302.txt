concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0302 final - 2014/0156 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\r\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\r\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\r\npossible after its adoption. \nArticle 78 of the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Contracting Parties should strengthen and broaden cooperation in the\r\nframework of the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, information\r\nservices, and\u00a0 research and innovation. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\r\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31\r\nto the EEA Agreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four\r\nfreedoms in order to extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties in the\r\narea of information services, and\u00a0 research and innovation.\nIn order to extend the cooperation of the\r\nContracting Parties to the EEA Agreement Regulation (EU) No 1316/2013 of the\r\nEuropean Parliament and of the Council of 11 December 2013 establishing the\r\nConnecting Europe Facility, amending Regulation (EU) No 913/2010 and repealing\r\nRegulations (EC) No 680/2007 and (EC) No 67/2010[1]\r\nneeds to be incorporated in the EEA Agreement.\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement should\r\ntherefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to take\r\nplace from 1 January 2014.\nIt should be noted that the EFTA States will\r\nonly participate in the telecommunications sector of the Connecting Europe\r\nFacility and Liechtenstein will be exempted from the participation in, and the\r\nfinancial contribution to, this programme.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\r\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\r\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\r\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\r\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\r\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2014/0156 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on
