The\r\nnegotiations with Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway were successfully concluded\r\nby the initialling of:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the Agreement;\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the Protocol; and\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 two additional protocols, namely: \n(a) the Additional Protocol to the Agreement between the European\r\nEconomic Community and the Republic of Iceland consequent to the accession of\r\nthe Republic of Croatia to the European Union; and \n(b) the Additional Protocol to the Agreement between the European\r\nEconomic Community and the Kingdom of Norway consequent to the accession of the\r\n  Republic of Croatia to the European Union. \nThese\r\ntwo additional protocols are part of a package with the current Agreement and\r\nProtocol but are contained in two separate proposals.\nThe proposed Agreement makes the Republic of Croatia a contracting party to the EEA Agreement and commits the EU to providing an authentic\r\nversion of the EEA Agreement in the new official language of the EU. The\r\nProtocol includes Croatia in the Norwegian Financial Mechanism.\nThe provisional\r\napplication of the Agreement and the Protocol is envisaged as of the date\r\nstipulated by an exchange of letters between the Parties, pending the\r\ncompletion of the relevant procedures for its formal conclusion and entry into\r\nforce.\nThe Commission has judged the results of\r\nthe negotiations to be satisfactory and asks the Council to adopt the attached\r\ndecision on the conclusion of the Agreement and the related Protocol.\n2014/0047 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the conclusion,
