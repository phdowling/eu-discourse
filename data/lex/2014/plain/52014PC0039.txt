The Annex to the Additional Agreement should\r\ntherefore be amended accordingly.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF THE\r\nCONSULTATIONS WITH INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe purpose of the proposal is simply to\r\namend the Additional Agreement in order to update the details of the competent Liechtenstein government agency and to take into account the amendments to Annex 7 and\r\nAnnex 12 to the Agreement.
