concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0262 final - 2014/0140 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant EU legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption and also allow for the participation of the EEA\nEFTA States in EEA relevant EU actions or programmes.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint\nCommittee (annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31 to\nthe EEA Agreementon cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms in\norder to extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties to the EEA Agreement\nto include cooperation concerning free movement of workers, coordination of\nsocial security systems and measures for migrants, including migrants from\nthird countries.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2014/0140 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union,\nin the EEA Joint Committee concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 46 and 48 in conjunction with Article 218(9) thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 of 28 November 1994 concerning arrangements for implementing the\nAgreement on the European Economic Area[1],\nand in particular Article 1(3) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Agreement on the\nEuropean Economic Area[2]\n(\u201cthe EEA Agreement\u201d) entered into force on 1 January 1994.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Pursuant to Article 98 of\nthe EEA Agreement, the EEA Joint Committee may decide to amend, inter alia, Protocol\n31 thereto.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement contains specific provisions on cooperation in specific fields\noutside the four freedoms.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is appropriate to\nextend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties to the EEA Agreement to\ninclude cooperation concerning free movement of workers, coordination of social\nsecurity systems and measures for migrants, including migrants from third\ncountries.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement should therefore be amended accordingly, in order to allow for this\nextended cooperation to take place from 1 January 2014.\n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The position of the Union in the EEA Joint Committee should be based on the attached draft Decision.\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be adopted, on behalf of\nthe European Union, in the EEA Joint Committee on the proposed amendment to Protocol\n31 to the EEA Agreement shall be based on the draft Decision of the\nEEA Joint Committee attached to this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThis Decision shall enter into force on the\nday of its adoption.\nDone at and\n2013 referred to in paragraph 12 as from 1 January 2012 and in the actions\nfunded from the budget line for the financial year 2014 referred to in\nparagraph 13 as from 1 January 2014\u201d.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In paragraphs 6 and 7, the words\n\u201cparagraphs 8 and 12\u201d are replaced by the words \u201cparagraphs 8, 12 and
