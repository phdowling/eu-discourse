European Union\r\n(hereinafter referred to as \"TFEU\"), this Agreement and any\r\nactivities entered into under it will in no way affect the powers vested in the\r\nMember States to undertake bilateral activities with the Faroes in the fields\r\nof science, technology, research and development, and to conclude, where\r\nappropriate, agreements to that end,\nHAVE AGREED established\r\nby, or referred to, in this Agreement and its Annexes, to Horizon 2020 as\r\nestablished by Regulation (EU) No 1291/2013[3]\r\nof the European Parliament and of the Council of 11 December 2013 establishing\r\nHorizon 2020 \u2013 the Framework Programme for Research and Innovation (2014-2020),\r\nand by Regulation (EU) No 1290/2013[4]\r\nlaying down the rules for the participation and dissemination in Horizon 2020,\r\nand by Council Decision 2013/743/EU[5],\r\nestablishing the Specific Programme Implementing Horizon 2020.\n1 bis.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 The Regulation (EC) No\r\n294/2008 of the European Parliament and of the Council as amended by the\r\nRegulation (EU) No 1292/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council\r\nshall apply to participation of Faroese legal entities in Knowledge and\r\nInnovation Communities[6].\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 All acts deriving from the\r\nacts referred to in of\r\nMember States of the Union, subject to the terms and conditions established by,\r\nor referred to, in Annexes I and II. \r\nthe amounts in commitment appropriations, in the\r\nstatement of expenditure of the draft budget of the European Union\r\ncorresponding to Horizon 2020. \n\u2013              \r\nthe estimated
