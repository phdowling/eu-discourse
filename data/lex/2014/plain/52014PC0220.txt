The act is\nincluded in Article 5 of Protocol 30 and changes are made reflecting the extended\nperiod 2014 to 2017. In addition, the draft JCD amends Article 5(3) in order to\nclarify that the EEA Statistical Programme is an annual programme. \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n2014/0123 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the
