In accordance\r\nwith Article 57(3) of the 10th EDF Financial Regulation, this\r\nproposal covers:\n\u2013     \r\nthe amount of the second instalment of the\r\ncontribution for 2014.\nIn accordance with Article 57(7) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the amount managed by the Commission and the amount\r\nmanaged by the EIB are specified separately.\nIn accordance with Article 145 of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the EIB has sent the Commission its updated estimates\r\nof commitments and payments under the instruments it manages.\nIn accordance with Article 58(2) of the\r\nFinancial Regulation calls for contributions should first use up the amounts\r\navailable from previous EDFs, one after the other. The calls for contributions\r\nin this proposal therefore concern amounts under the 9th EDF for the EIB and\r\nthe 10th EDF for the Commission.\nIn accordance with Article 57(3) of the 10th\r\nEDF Financial Regulation, the Council must decide on this proposal at the\r\nlatest 21 calendar days following the presentation by the Commission of its\r\nproposal and Member States must pay the second instalment at the latest 21\r\ncalendar days following the date on which the Council decision was notified to\r\nthem.\nArticle 60(1) of the Financial Regulation\r\nprovides that a Member State which
