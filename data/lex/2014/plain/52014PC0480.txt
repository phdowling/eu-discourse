The negotiations with Indonesia were successfully concluded.\nThe proposed Protocol incorporates\r\nthe Republic of Croatia as contracting party to the Agreement. The texts of the Agreement and the Final Act in the Croatian\r\nlanguage are annexed to the Protocol and will become authentic under the same\r\nconditions as other language versions of the Agreement.\nThe Protocol will apply provisionally as of\r\nthe date of its signature.\nThe Commission considers the outcome of the\r\nnegotiations satisfactory and requests the Council to authorise the signature,\r\non behalf of the European Union and its Member States, and provisional\r\napplication of the Protocol.\n2014/0219 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the signing, on behalf of the European\r\nUnion and its Member States, and provisional application of the Accession\r\nProtocol to the Framework Agreement on Comprehensive Partnership and\r\nCooperation between the European Community and its Member States, of the one\r\npart, and the Republic of Indonesia, of the other part, to take\r\naccount of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\r\nof the European Union, and in particular Articles 207 and 209, in conjunction\r\nwith Article 218(5) thereof,\nHaving regard to the Act of Accession of\r\nthe Republic of Croatia, and in particular Article 6 (2) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\r\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Under the terms of Article\r\n6(2) of the Act of Accession of the Republic of Croatia, the accession of the\r\nRepublic of Croatia to the Framework Agreement on Comprehensive Partnership and\r\nCooperation between the European Community and its Member States, of the one\r\npart, and the Republic of Indonesia, of the other part[2] (\"the\r\nAgreement\") is to be agreed by means of a protocol to the Agreement. shall enter into force on the\r\nday of its adoption.\nDone at
