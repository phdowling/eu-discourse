"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52014PC0090\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the signing, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, and provisional application of an Agreement on the participation of the Republic of Croatia in the European Economic Area and the related Protocol, to take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union /* COM/2014/090 final - 2014/0048 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nThe Agreement on the European Economic\r\nArea, hereinafter referred to as \u2018the EEA Agreement\u2019, was signed on 2 May 1992\r\nand entered into force on 1 January 1994.\nAccording to the Act of Accession of the Republic of Croatia, Croatia undertakes to accede, under the conditions laid down in the Act,\r\nto the Agreement on the European Economic Area, in accordance with Article 128\r\nof that Agreement.\nThe attached proposal creates the legal\r\ninstrument for the signature and provisional application of the Agreement on\r\nthe participation of the Republic of Croatia in the European Economic Area (\u2018the\r\nAgreement\u2019) and of the related Additional Protocol to\r\nthe Agreement between the Kingdom of Norway and the European Union on a\r\nNorwegian Financial Mechanism for the period 2009-2014 consequent to the\r\nparticipation of the Republic of Croatia in the European Economic Area (\u2018the Protocol\u2019)\r\nto take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European\r\nUnion.\nOn 4 September 2012[1], the\r\nCouncil authorised the Commission to open negotiations with the Republic of Iceland, the Principality of Liechtenstein and the Kingdom of Norway to amend the\r\nEEA Agreement and the Agreement between the Kingdom of Norway and the European\r\nUnion on a Norwegian Financial Mechanism for the period 2009-2014. The\r\nnegotiations with Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway were successfully concluded\r\nby the initialling of:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the Agreement;\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 the Protocol; and\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 two additional protocols, namely: \n(a) the Additional Protocol to the Agreement between the European\r\nEconomic Community and the Republic of Iceland consequent to the accession of\r\nthe Republic of Croatia to the European Union; and \n(b) the Additional Protocol to the Agreement between the European\r\nEconomic Community and the Kingdom of Norway consequent to the accession of the\r\n  Republic of Croatia to the European Union. \nThese\r\ntwo additional protocols are part of a package with the current Agreement and\r\nProtocol but are contained in two separate proposals.\nThe proposed Agreement makes the Republic of   Croatia a contracting party to the EEA Agreement and commits the EU to providing\r\nan authentic version of the EEA Agreement in the new official language of the\r\nEU. The Protocol includes Croatia in the Norwegian Financial Mechanism.\nThe provisional application of the\r\nAgreement and the Protocol is envisaged as of the date stipulated by an\r\nexchange of letters between the Parties, pending the completion of the relevant\r\nprocedures for its formal conclusion and entry into force.\nThe Commission has judged the results of\r\nthe negotiations to be satisfactory and asks the Council to adopt the attached\r\ndecision on the signature and provisional application of the Agreement.\n2014/0048 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the signing,
