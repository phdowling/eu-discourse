"\nText\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n|\n\n\n\n52014PC0092\n\nProposal for a COUNCIL DECISION on the signing, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, and provisional application of an Additional Protocol to the Agreement between the European Economic Community and the Kingdom of Norway, to take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union /* COM/2014/092 final - 2014/0049 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\nThe Agreement between the European Economic\r\nCommunity and the Kingdom of Norway, hereinafter referred to as \u2018the Agreement\u2019,\r\nwas signed on 14 May 1973 in Brussels and entered into force on 1 July 1973.\nThe attached proposal creates the legal\r\ninstrument for the signature and provisional application of an Additional\r\nProtocol to the Agreement between the European Economic Community and the\r\nKingdom of Norway, to take account of the accession of the Republic of Croatia\r\nto the European Union (hereinafter referred to as \u2018the Additional Protocol\u2019).\nAccording to the Act of\r\nAccession of the Republic of Croatia, Croatia shall accede to the international\r\nagreements signed or concluded by the European Union and its Member States by means of a protocol to those agreements.\nOn 24 September 2012[1], the\r\nCouncil authorised the Commission to open negotiations with the third countries\r\nconcerned to conclude the relevant protocols. Negotiations with Norway were\r\nconducted in the framework of the negotiations for the adaptation of the\r\nAgreement on the European Economic Area and the Agreement between the Kingdom\r\nof Norway and the European Union on a Norwegian Financial Mechanism for the\r\nperiod 2009-2014, in view of the enlargement of the European Union to include\r\nCroatia[2],\r\nand were successfully concluded by the initialling of the Additional Protocol.\nThe proposed Additional\r\nProtocol makes the Republic of Croatia a contracting party to the Agreement and\r\ncommits the EU to providing an authentic version of the Agreement in the new\r\nofficial language of the EU. It also provides for additional fish trade\r\nconcessions for Norway, given Croatia\u2019s accession to the European Economic\r\nArea.\nIn accordance with\r\nArticle 4 of the Additional Protocol and pending the completion of the relevant\r\nprocedures for its formal conclusion and entry into force, the provisional\r\napplication of the Protocol should begin as of the first day of the third month\r\nfollowing the deposit of the last notification of the Parties to this effect.\nThe Commission has judged the results of\r\nthe negotiations to be satisfactory and asks the Council to adopt the attached\r\ndecision on the signature and provisional application of the Additional\r\nProtocol.\n2014/0049 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the signing, on behalf of the European\r\nUnion and its Member States, and provisional application of an Additional\r\nProtocol to the Agreement between the European Economic Community and the\r\nKingdom of Norway, to take account is hereby approved on behalf of the Union and\r\nthe Member States, subject to the conclusion of the Additional Protocol.\nThe text of the Additional Protocol is\r\nattached to this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThe Council General Secretariat shall\r\nestablish the instrument of full powers to sign the Additional Protocol,\r\nsubject to its conclusion, for the person(s) indicated by the negotiator of the\r\nProtocol.\nArticle 3\nSubject to its\r\nconclusion at a later date and pending its entry into force, the Additional\r\nProtocol shall be applied on a provisional basis from\r\nthe first day of the third month following the deposit of the last notification\r\nto this effect, in accordance with Article 4 of the Protocol.\nDone at
