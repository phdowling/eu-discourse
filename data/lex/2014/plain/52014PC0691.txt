The\r\nCouncil decision of 14 September 2012[2]\r\nauthorised the Commission to open negotiations with the third countries\r\nconcerned, in order to conclude the relevant protocols.\nThe Agreement on Maritime Transport between\r\nthe European Community and its Member States and the Government of the People\u2019s\r\nRepublic of China was signed in Brussels on 6 December 2002, and concluded by\r\nCouncil Decision of 28 January 2008 and entered into force on 1 March 2008. \nConsequently, the Commission has negotiated\r\na Protocol on the basis of negotiating directives adopted by the Council on 14\r\nSeptember 2012, and in consultation with a special committee appointed by the\r\nCouncil. The draft Protocol was initialled by the Commission and the Chinese\r\nrepresentatives in Brussels on 20 June 2014. \nFollowing negotiations with the People\u2019s\r\nRepublic of China, the following distinct proposals are submitted:\n(i)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Proposal for a Council Decision on the signature of a\r\nProtocol to the Agreement (hereinafter referred to as 'the Protocol') \n(ii) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Proposal for a Council Decision on the conclusion of the\r\nProtocol, to be taken up by the Council in due time following the signature of\r\nthe Protocol.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\r\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nNegotiations with the People's Republic of China were successfully concluded by the initialling of the Protocol on 20 June 2014.\u00a0 \n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\r\nPROPOSAL\nThe Protocol makes the Republic of Croatia a party to the Agreement and provides for the necessary linguistic\r\nadaptations of the Agreement that ensue from the accession of the Republic of   Croatia. No Changes to the substance of the agreement are made.\nThe Commission asks the Council to\r\nauthorise the signature, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States,\r\nof a Protocol to the Agreement on Maritime Transport between the European\r\nCommunity and its Member States, of the one part, and the Government of the\r\nPeople\u2019s Republic of China, of the other part, to take account of Croatia's\r\naccession to the EU.\u00a0 Following the signature of the protocol, the second\r\nproposal, on the conclusion of the Protocol, will be considered by the Council\r\nin due
