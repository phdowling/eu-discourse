It called for an immediate\r\nend to the violence, full respect of human rights and fundamental freedoms. It\r\ncalled upon the Ukrainian Government to exercise maximum restraint and\r\nopposition leaders to distance themselves from those who resort to radical\r\naction, including violence.\n(2)              \r\nOn \u2026 February 2014, the Council adopted Decision\r\n2014/\u2026/CFSP concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Ukraine.\n(3)              \r\nDecision 2014/\u2026/CFSP provides for the freezing\r\nof funds and economic resources of certain persons\r\nresponsible for violations of human rights, violence or use of excessive force. These natural or legal persons, entities and bodies are listed in\r\nthe Annex to the Decision.\n(4)              \r\nFurther action by the Union is needed in order\r\nto implement Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP.\n(5)              \r\nThe High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and the\r\nEuropean Commission should make a proposal for a Regulation concerning\r\nrestrictive measures in view of the situation in Ukraine.\n2014/0062 (NLE)\nJoint Proposal for a\nCOUNCIL REGULATION\nconcerning restrictive measures in view of\r\nthe situation in Ukraine \nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the\r\nFunctioning of the European Union, and in particular Article 215 thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP[1] of \u2026 concerning\r\nrestrictive measures in view of the situation in Ukraine,\nHaving regard to the joint proposal from the\r\nHigh Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and\r\nthe Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On 20 February 2014, the\r\nCouncil condemned in the strongest terms all use of violence in Ukraine. It called for an immediate end to the violence, full respect of human rights and\r\nfundamental freedoms in Ukraine. It called upon the Ukrainian Government to\r\nexercise maximum restraint and opposition leaders to distance themselves from\r\nthose who resort to radical action, including violence.\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 On \u2026 February 2014, the\r\nCouncil adopted Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP concerning restrictive measures in view of\r\nthe situation in Ukraine.\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Decision 2014/\u2026/CFSP\r\nprovides for the freezing of funds and economic resources of certain persons responsible for violations of human rights, violence or use of excessive\r\nforce. These natural or legal persons, entities and\r\nbodies are listed in the Annex to the Decision.\n(4)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 These measures fall within\r\nthe scope of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union and,\r\ntherefore, notably with a view to ensuring their uniform application by\r\neconomic operators in all Member States, regulatory action at the level of the Union is necessary in order to implement them.\n(5)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 This Regulation respects\r\nthe fundamental rights and observes the principles recognised in particular by\r\nthe Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union and notably the right\r\nto an effective remedy and to a fair trial and the right to the protection of\r\npersonal data. This Regulation should be applied in accordance with those\r\nrights. \n(6)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In order to ensure uniform\r\nconditions for the implementation of this Regulation, implementing powers\r\nshould be conferred on the Commission. Those powers should be exercised in\r\naccordance with Regulation (EU) No 182/2011[2].\n(7)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 For the implementation of\r\nthis Regulation, and in order to create maximum legal certainty within the\r\nUnion, the names and other relevant data concerning natural and legal persons,\r\nentities and bodies whose funds and economic resources must be frozen in\r\naccordance with this Regulation, must be made public. Any processing of\r\npersonal data should comply with Regulation (EC) No 45/2001[3] and Directive 95/46/EC[4].\n(8)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 In order to ensure that\r\nthe measures provided for in this Regulation are effective, it should enter\r\ninto force immediately,\nHAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:\nArticle 1\nFor the purposes of this Regulation, the\r\nfollowing definitions shall apply:\n(a)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018claim\u2019 means any claim, whether\r\nasserted by legal proceedings or not, made before or after the date of entry\r\ninto force of this Regulation, under or in connection with a contract or\r\ntransaction, and includes in particular: \n(i) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 a claim for performance of any\r\nobligation arising under or in connection with a contract or transaction; \n(ii) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 a claim for extension or payment of a\r\nbond, financial guarantee or indemnity of whatever form; \n(iii) \u00a0\u00a0 a claim for compensation in respect\r\nof a contract or transaction; \n(iv) \u00a0\u00a0 a counterclaim; \n(v) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 a claim for the recognition or\r\nenforcement, including by the procedure of exequatur, of a judgment, an\r\narbitration award or an equivalent decision, wherever made or given;\n(b)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018contract or transaction\u2019 means\r\nany transaction of whatever form and whatever the applicable law, whether\r\ncomprising one or more contracts or similar obligations made between the same\r\nor different parties; \n(e)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 'freezing of economic resources'\r\nmeans preventing the use of economic resources to obtain funds, goods or\r\nservices in any way, including, but not limited to, by selling, hiring or\r\nmortgaging them; \n(f) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018freezing of funds\u2019 means\r\npreventing any move, transfer, alteration, use of, access to, or dealing with\r\nfunds in any way that would result in any change in their volume, amount,\r\nlocation, ownership, possession, character, destination or other change that\r\nwould enable the funds to be used, including portfolio management;\n(g)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018funds\u2019 means financial assets\r\nand benefits of every kind, including, but not limited to: \n(i) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 cash, cheques, claims on money, drafts,\r\nmoney orders and other payment instruments; \n(ii) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 deposits with financial institutions\r\nor other entities, balances on accounts, debts and debt obligations; \n(iii) \u00a0\u00a0 publicly-and privately-traded\r\nsecurities and debt instruments, including stocks and shares, certificates\r\nrepresenting securities, bonds, notes, warrants, debentures and derivatives\r\ncontracts; \n(iv) \u00a0\u00a0 interest, dividends or other income on\r\nor value accruing from or generated by assets; \n(v) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 credit, right of set-off, guarantees,\r\nperformance bonds or other financial commitments; \n(vi) \u00a0\u00a0 letters of credit, bills of lading,\r\nbills of sale; and \n(vii) \u00a0 documents showing evidence of an\r\ninterest in funds or financial resources; \n(h)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018territory of the Union\u2019 means the territories of the Member States to which the Treaty is applicable, under\r\nthe conditions laid down in the Treaty, including their airspace;\n(i)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 \u2018transfer of funds\u2019 means: \n(i) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 any transaction carried out on behalf\r\nof a payer through a payment service provider by electronic means, with a view\r\nto making funds available to a payee at a payment service provider,\r\nirrespective of whether the payer and the payee are the same person. The terms\r\npayer, payee and payment service provider have the same meaning as in Directive\r\n2007/64/EC[5];\r\n\n(ii) \u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 any transaction by non-electronic\r\nmeans such as in cash, cheques or accountancy orders, with a view to making\r\nfunds available to a payee irrespective of whether the payer and the payee are\r\nthe same person.\nArticle
