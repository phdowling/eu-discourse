Negotiations with Iceland were successfully concluded by the initialling of the Additional Protocol.\nThe proposed Additional\nProtocol makes the Republic of Croatia a contracting party to the Agreement and\ncommits the EU to providing an authentic version of the Agreement in the new\nofficial language of the EU. It also provides for additional fish trade\nconcessions for Iceland, given Croatia\u2019s accession to the European Economic\nArea.\nThe Additional Protocol was signed in Brussels on (...). The Council is asked to adopt the decision on the conclusion of the\nAdditional Protocol, after obtaining the consent of the European Parliament.\n2014/0046 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non
