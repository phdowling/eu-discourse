concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0198 final - 2014/0112 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal\nsecurity and homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to\nintegrate all the relevant Union legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as\npossible after its adoption and also allow for the participation of the EEA\nEFTA States in EEA relevant EU actions, activities or programmes.\n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee\n(annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms.\nThis shall be done via inclusion to the EEA\nAgreement of Regulation (EU) No 1295/2013 of the European Parliament and of the\nCouncil of 11 December 2013 establishing the Creative Europe Programme (2014 to\n2020) and repealing Decisions No 1718/2006/EC, No 1855/2006/EC and No\n1041/2009/EC[1].\nIt shall also be noted that Liechtenstein shall be exempted from the participation in, and the financial contribution\nto, this programme.\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement should\ntherefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to take\nplace from 1 January 2014.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision\nof the EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 BUDGETARY IMPLICATION \nIt is proposed that Protocol 31 to the Agreement\non the EEA be amended in order to allow EEA/EFTA States to participate in the\nprogramme and to contribute financially to it as stipulated in the above\nmentioned Regulation (EU) No 1295/2013.\n2014/0112 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be adopted, on behalf\nof the European Union, in the EEA Joint Committee concerning an amendment to\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the\nfour freedoms\n\u00a0\nTHE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,\nHaving regard to the Treaty on the Functioning\nof the European Union, in particular \nArticle 166(4), the first indent of Article\n167(5), Article 173(3) and 218 (9) thereof,\nHaving regard to Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 of 28 November 1994 concerning arrangements for implementing the\nAgreement on the European Economic Area[2],\nand in particular Article 1(3) thereof,\nHaving regard to the proposal from the\nEuropean Commission,\nWhereas:\n(1)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the\nAgreement on the European Economic Area, (hereinafter referred to as \"the\nEEA Agreement\") contains specific provisions and arrangements concerning\ncooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms,\n(2)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 It is appropriate to\nextend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties to the EEA Agreement to\ninclude Regulation (EU) No 1295/2013 of the European Parliament and of the\nCouncil of 11 December 2013 establishing the Creative Europe Programme (2014 to\n2020) and repealing Decisions No 1718/2006/EC, No 1855/2006/EC and No 1041/2009/EC[3],\n(3)\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement should therefore be amended in order to allow for this extended\ncooperation to take place from 1 January 2014.\nHAS ADOPTED THIS DECISION: \nArticle 1\nThe position to be taken by the Union in the EEA Joint Committee on the proposed amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement shall be based on the draft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee\nattached to this Decision.\nArticle 2\nThis\nDecision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption.\nDone at
