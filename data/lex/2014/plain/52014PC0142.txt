concerning an amendment to Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement, on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms /* COM/2014/0142 final - 2014/0081 (NLE) */\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nEXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM\n1.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL\nIn order to ensure the requisite legal security\nand homogeneity of the Internal Market, the EEA Joint Committee is to integrate\nall the relevant Union legislation into the EEA Agreement as soon as possible\nafter its adoption and also allow for the participation of the EEA EFTA States\nin EEA relevant EU actions, activities or programmes.\nArticle 78 of the EEA Agreement provides that\nthe Contracting Parties shall strengthen and broaden cooperation in the framework\nof the Union\u2019s activities in the fields of, inter alia, education, training and\nyouth. \n2.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 RESULTS OF CONSULTATIONS\nWITH THE INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENTS\nThe draft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee\n(annexed to the proposed Council Decision) aims to amend Protocol 31 to the EEA\nAgreement on cooperation in specific fields outside the four freedoms in order\nto extend the cooperation of the Contracting Parties in the area of education,\ntraining and youth.\nIt is therefore needed to incorporate in the\nEEA Agreement Regulation (EU) No 1288/2013 of the European Parliament and of\nthe Council of 11 December 2013 establishing \u2018Erasmus+\u2019: the Union programme\nfor education, training, youth and sport and repealing Decisions No\n1719/2006/EC, No 1720/2006/EC and No 1298/2008/EC[1].\nProtocol 31 to the EEA Agreement should\ntherefore be amended in order to allow for this extended cooperation to take\nplace from 1 January 2014.\n3.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE\nPROPOSAL\nArticle 1(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No\n2894/94 concerning arrangements for implementing the EEA Agreement provides\nthat the Council establishes the position to be adopted on the Union\u2019s behalf on such Decisions, on a proposal from the Commission. \nThe Commission submits the Draft Decision of\nthe EEA Joint Committee for adoption by the Council as the Union\u2019s position.\nThe Commission would hope to be able to present it in the EEA Joint Committee\nat the earliest possible opportunity.\n4.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0 BUDGETARY IMPLICATION \nIt is proposed\nthat Protocol 31 to the Agreement on the EEA be amended in order to allow\nEEA/EFTA States to participate in the programme and to contribute financially\nto it as indicated in the decision.\n2014/0081 (NLE)\nProposal for a\nCOUNCIL DECISION\non the position to be
