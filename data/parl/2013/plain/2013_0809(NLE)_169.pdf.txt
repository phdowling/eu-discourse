2013/0809(NLE) - 11/12/2013 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament adopted by 534 votes to 32, with 37 abstentions, a decision in which it delivered a favourable opinion on the
Councils nomination of Danièle LAMARQUE (FR) as a Member of the Court of Auditors after having agreed that his credentials fulfilled the
requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union.

2013/0809(NLE) - 17/12/2013 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint five members of the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2013/772/EC appointing five members of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: the terms of office of Mr Gijs DE VRIES, Mr Henri GRETHEN, Mr Michel CRETIN, Mr Ioannis SARMAS and Mr David BOSTOCK
are due to expire on 31 December 2013.
New appointments have therefore been made to replace these members.
The Council has appointed, amongst others, Ms Danièle LAMARQUE (FR) for the period from 1 January 2014 to 31 December 2019.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 17/12/2013.
