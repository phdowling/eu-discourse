2013/2072(INS) - 03/07/2013 Final act
PURPOSE: to elect the European Ombudsman.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Decision 2013/377/EU, Euratom of the European Parliament electing the European Ombudsman.
CONTENT: having regard to the vote of 3 July 2013, Parliament decided to elect Emily OREILLY to exercise the function of European
Ombudsman from 1 October 2013 until the end of the parliamentary term.

2013/2072(INS) - 03/07/2013 Text adopted by Parliament, single reading
The European Parliament decided to elect, by secret ballot, Emily OREILLY to exercise the function of European Ombudsman from 1 October
2013 until the end of the parliamentary term.
It requests Emily O'REILLY to take an oath before the Court of Justice.
