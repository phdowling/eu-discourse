2013/0805(NLE) - 29/05/2013 Committee report tabled for plenary, single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report drafted by Inés AYALA SENDER (S&D, ES) in which it calls on the European
Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Council's nomination of George PUFAN (RO) as a Member of the Court of Auditors. George
PUFAN fulfils the conditions laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the EU (TFEU).

2013/0805(NLE) - 12/06/2013 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament delivered a favourable opinion on the Council's nomination of George PUFAN (RO) as a Member of the Court of
Auditors with 334 votes in favour, 290 against and 48 abstentions.
George PUFAN fulfils the conditions laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the EU (TFEU).

2013/0805(NLE) - 25/06/2013 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a member of the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2013/626/EU appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: the term of office of Mr Ovidiu ISPIR is due to expire on 30 June 2013. The Council decided to appoint Mr George PUFAN (RO) to
replace Mr ISPIR as a member of the Court of Auditors for the period from 1 July 2013 to 30 June 2019.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 25/06/2013.
