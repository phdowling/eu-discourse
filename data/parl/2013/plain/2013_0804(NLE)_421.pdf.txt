2013/0804(NLE) - 29/05/2013 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report drafted by Inés AYALA SENDER (S&D, ES) in which it calls on the European
Parliament to deliver a negative opinion on the Council's nomination of Neven MATES (HR) as a Member of the Court of Auditors. The
committee advises Parliament against endorsing Neven MATESs candidacy for this post.

2013/0804(NLE) - 12/06/2013 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament delivered a negative opinion on the Council's nomination of Neven MATES (HR) as a Member of the Court of
Auditors. Mr MATESs appointment was rejected by 396 votes to 231, with 45 abstentions.
To recap, Mr MATES was evaluated by the competent parliamentary committee in accordance with Article 286(2). Following his hearing, the
committee in question concluded his credentials were inappropriate for the post.

2013/0804(NLE) - 09/07/2013 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a member of the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2013/374/EU appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: following the accession of Croatia to the European Union, the Council decided to appoint Mr Neven MATES (HR) as a member of
the Court of Auditors for a term of office of six years, from 15 July 2013 to 14 July 2019. It should be noted that Parliament had delivered a
negative opinion of this appointment.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 09/07/2013.
