2015/0803(NLE) - 22/06/2015 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Igor OLTES (Greens/EFA, SI).
It called on the European Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Bettina Michelle JAKOBSEN as a Member
of the Court of Auditors.
Following the hearing of the candidate held on 17 June 2015, the Committee on Budgetary Control considered that Bettina Michelle
JAKOBSEN fulfilled the requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).

2015/0803(NLE) - 07/07/2015 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament decided by 558 votes to 30, with 108 abstentions, to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of
Bettina Michelle JAKOBSEN as a Member of the Court of Auditors.
Bettina Michelle JAKOBSEN fulfilled the requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union
(TFEU).

2015/0803(NLE) - 20/07/2015 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a Danish member to the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision (EU) 2015/1209 appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: with this Decision, the Council decided to appoint Bettina Michelle JAKOBSEN as a member of the Court of Auditors for the period
from 1 September 2015 to 28 February 2018.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: the Decision shall enter into force on 20.7.2015.
