2015/0903(NLE) - 16/12/2015 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament approved by 532 votes to 101, with 55 abstentions, the proposal for the extension of the term of office of Andrea
Enria as Chairperson of the European Banking Authority (EBA) by another five-year term.
To recall, on 8 September 2015, the Board of Supervisors of the EBA proposed to extend the term of office of Andrea Enria, the incumbent
Chairperson of the EBA, by another five-year term, and informed the European Parliament accordingly.
On 17 November 2015, the Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs held a hearing with Andrea Enria at which he made an opening
statement and then responded to questions from the members of the Committee.
