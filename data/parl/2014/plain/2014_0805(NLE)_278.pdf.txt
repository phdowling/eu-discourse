2014/0805(NLE) - 16/07/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
In accordance with the second paragraph of Article 246 of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU), and following the
resignation of Olli Rehn, Janusz Lewandowski, Antonio Tajani and Viviane Reding as Members of the Commission, with effect from 30 June
2014, the European Parliament approved the appointment Jyrki Katainen (FI), Jacek Dominik (PL), Ferdinando Nelli Feroci (IT) and Martine
Reicherts (LU) as Members of the Commission for the remainder of the Commissions term of office until 31 October 2014.
These Members were heard on the 14 July 2014 by the Parliamentary committees and statements of evaluation were drawn up following
those hearings.

2014/0805(NLE) - 17/07/2014 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a Member of the Commission.
NON LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2014/470/EU taken by common accord with the President of the Commission, appointing a
Member of the European Commission.
CONTENT: by common accord with Mr José Manuel DURÃO BARROSO, President of the Commission, the Council appoints Mr Ferdinando
NELLI FEROCI (IT) as Member of the Commission for the remainder of the term of office which runs until 31 October 2014.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 17.07.2014.
