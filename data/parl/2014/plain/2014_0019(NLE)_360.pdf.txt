2014/0019(NLE) - 31/01/2014 Initial legislative proposal
PURPOSE: to conclude the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one
part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union.
PROPOSED ACT: Council Decision.
ROLE OF THE EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT: Council may adopt the act only if Parliament has given its consent to the act.
BACKGROUND: on 24 September 2012, the Council authorised the Commission to open negotiations with the Republic of Korea in order to
conclude an Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and the
Republic of Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union.
Those negotiations were successfully completed by initialling the Protocol on 8 November 2013.
It is now necessary to conclude this Protocol on behalf of the European Union.
LEGAL BASIS: Articles 91, 100(2), 167(3), 207 and 218(6), second subparagraph, point (a)(v) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the
European Union (TFEU) as well as the Act of Accession of Croatia.
CONTENT: under this proposal, the Council is invited to approve, on behalf of the European Union, the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade

Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the
accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union.
The text of the Protocol is attached to the proposal.
A parallel decision is proposed for the signature and the provisional application of the Protocol on behalf of the European Union and its
Member States.

2014/0019(NLE) - 06/03/2014 Legislative proposal
PURPOSE: to conclude the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one
part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union.
PROPOSED ACT: Council Decision.
ROLE OF THE EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT: Council may adopt the act only if Parliament has given its consent to the act.
BACKGROUND: in accordance with a Council decision, the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union
and its Member States, of the one part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union
was signed and applied on a provisional basis from 1 July 2013, pending the completion of the procedures for its conclusion.
The Additional Protocol should be approved by the European Union.
LEGAL BASIS: Articles 91, 100(2), 167(3), 207 and 218(6), second subparagraph, point (a)(v) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the
European Union (TFEU) as well as the Act of Accession of Croatia.
CONTENT: under this proposal, the Council is invited to approve, on behalf of the European Union, the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade
Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the
accession of the Republic of Croatia to the European Union.
The President of the Council shall designate the person(s) empowered to proceed, on behalf of the Union and its Member States, to give the
notification referred to in Article 9 of the Additional Protocol regarding completion of the internal procedures for the entry into force. The content
of the agreement is identical to that of the Commissions proposal (please refer to the summary dated 31/01/2014 of the initial legislative
proposal).

2014/0019(NLE) - 29/09/2014 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on International Trade adopted the report by Jan Zahradil (ECR, CZ) in which it recommended the European Parliament to
give its consent to the draft Council decision on the conclusion, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, of the Additional
Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the
other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union (EU).
The aim is to ensure, in accordance with the Act of Accession of Croatia to the EU, that this Member State continues to fully participate in the
EU-Korea FTA that remains in force.
The European Parliament's consent to this Council Decision is a mandatory component in the completion of the ratification process of the
Additional Protocol, and it also contributes to safeguarding the integrity of the Union's internal market.

2014/0019(NLE) - 22/10/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament adopted by 524 votes to 53, with 106 abstentions, a legislative resolution on the draft Council decision on the
conclusion, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, of the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the
European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of
Croatia to the European Union.
Parliament gave its consent to the conclusion of the Additional Protocol.

2014/0019(NLE) - 10/11/2014 Final act
PURPOSE: to conclude the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one
part, and South Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2014/789/EU on the conclusion, on behalf of the European Union and its Member States, of the
Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of
Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union.
BACKGROUND: in accordance with Council Decision 2014/257/EU, the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the
European Union and its Member States, of the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of
Croatia to the European Union was signed, and has been applied provisionally as from 1 July 2013, pending the completion of the procedures
for its conclusion.
The Additional Protocol should now be approved on behalf of the European Union.

CONTENT: with this Decision, the Additional Protocol to the Free Trade Agreement between the European Union and its Member States, of
the one part, and the Republic of Korea, of the other part, to take account of the accession of Croatia to the European Union is hereby
approved on behalf of the Union and its Member States.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: the Decision shall enter into force on 14.11.2014.
