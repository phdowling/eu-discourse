2014/2011(REG) - 18/03/2014 Committee report tabled for plenary, single reading
The Committee on Constitutional Affairs adopted the report by György SCHÖPFLIN (EPP, HU) on amendment of Parliament's Rules of
Procedure so as to allow for the possibility of electronic signatures.
Members proposed to introduce a new Rule 148a on the electronic handling of documents: Parliament documents may be prepared, signed
and distributed in electronic form. The Bureau should decide on the technical specifications and on the presentation of the electronic form.

2014/2011(REG) - 16/04/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, single reading
The European Parliament decided by 660 votes to 9, with 5 abstentions, to amend Parliament's Rules of Procedure so as to allow for the
possibility of electronic signatures.
A new Rule 148a on the electronic handling of documents has been introduced : it stipulates that Parliament documents may be prepared,
signed and distributed in electronic form. The Bureau should decide on the technical specifications and on the presentation of the electronic
form.
