2014/2058(INS) - 15/07/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, single reading
The European Parliament has elected Jean-Claude Juncker as President of the Commission for the term of office running from 1 November
2014 until 31 October 2019.
The total number of votes cast were:
729
10 of which were non valid.
The results of the vote were as follows:
422 members voted in favour,
250 against,
47 abstained.
In accordance with European Council Decision 2014/414/EU of 27 June 2014, the European Council proposed Jean-Claude Juncker as
candidate for President of the Commission.
Parliament instructed its President to ask the Council and Jean-Claude Juncker to propose by common accord the nominees for appointment
as members of the Commission.
