2014/0802(NLE) - 19/02/2014 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Inés AYALA SENDER (S&D, ES).
The committee called on the European Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Oskar HERICS (AT) as a
Member of the Court of Auditors.
Following its meeting of 17 February 2014, the Committee on Budgetary Control considered that Oskar Herics fulfilled the requirements laid
down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).

2014/0802(NLE) - 25/02/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament adopted by 472 votes to 39, with 48 abstentions, a decision in which it gave a favourable opinion on the Councils
nomination of Oskar HERICS as a Member of the Court of Auditors after he fulfilled the requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty
on the Functioning of the European Union.

2014/0802(NLE) - 27/02/2014 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a member of the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2014/108/EU appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: the term of office of Mr Harald WÖGERBAUER is due to expire on 28 February 2014. The Council decided to appoint Mr Oskar
HERICS (AT) to replace Mr WÖGERBAUER as a member of the Court of Auditors for the period from 1 March 2014 to 29 February 2020.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 27/02.2014.
