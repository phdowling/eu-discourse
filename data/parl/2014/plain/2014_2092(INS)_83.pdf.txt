2014/2092(INS) - 16/12/2014 Text adopted by Parliament, single reading
The European Parliament decided by 569 votes to 66, with 43 abstentions, to elect Emily O'REILLY to exercise the function of European
Ombudsman until the end of the parliamentary term. It requested Emily O'REILLY to take an oath before the Court of Justice.

2014/2092(INS) - 16/12/2014 Final act
PURPOSE: to elect the European Ombudsman.
NON LEGISLATIVE ACT: Decision n° 949/2014/EU of the European Parliament, Euratom, electing the European Ombudsman.

CONTENT: following the vote taken during the plenary sitting of 16.12.2014, the European Parliament decided to elect Emily O'REILLY to
exercise the function of European Ombudsman until the end of the parliamentary term.
