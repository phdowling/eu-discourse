2016/0807(NLE) - 08/09/2016 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Igor OLTES (Greens/EFA, SI).
The committee called on the European Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Lazaros Stavrou LAZAROU
(CY) as a Member of the Court of Auditors.
Following his hearing which was held on 5 September 2016, the Committee on Budgetary Control considered that the candidate met the
requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).

2016/0807(NLE) - 13/09/2016 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament adopted by 520 votes to 48, with 120 abstentions, a decision on the nomination of Lazaros Stavrou LAZAROU (CY)
as a Member of the Court of Auditors, having met the requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the
European Union (TFEU).

2016/0807(NLE) - 29/09/2016 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a Cypriot member to the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision (EU, Euratom) 2016/1763 appointing three members of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: with this Decision, the Council decided to appoint Lazaros S. LAZAROU as a member of the Court of Auditors for the period from
2 November 2016 to 1 November 2022.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: the Decision shall enter into force on 29.9.2016.
