2016/0805(NLE) - 25/05/2016 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Bart STAES (Greens/EFA, BE) in which it recommended the European Parliament
to give a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Rimantas adius as a Member of the Court of Auditors.
Parliaments Committee on Budgetary Control evaluated the credentials of the nominee, in particular in view of the requirements laid down in
Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union. Then at its meeting of 23 May 2016, the committee heard the Councils
nominee for membership of the Court of Auditors.
The candidate's answers to questions from members as regards professional experience, the guarantees of independence and the
performance of duties are annexed to the report.

2016/0805(NLE) - 07/06/2016 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
In line with the recommendation made by the Committee on Budget Control, the European Parliament decided by 498 to 120, with 53
abstentions, to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Rimantas adius as a Lithuanian Member of the Court of Auditors.

2016/0805(NLE) - 16/06/2016 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint a Lithuanian member to the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision 2016/981 (EU, Euratom) appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: With this Decision, the Council decides to appoint Rimantas ADIUS as the Lithuanian Member of the Court of Auditors for the
period 16.6.2016 to 15.6.2022.
He will replace Ms Rasa BUDBERGYT? whose term of office expired on 6 May 2016.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: 16.6.2016.
