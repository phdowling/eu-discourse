2016/0806(NLE) - 08/09/2016 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Igor OLTES (Greens/EFA, SI).
The committee called on the European Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Leo BRINCAT (MT) as a
Member of the Court of Auditors.
Following his hearing which was held on 5 September 2016, the Committee on Budgetary Control considered that the candidate met the
requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).

2016/0806(NLE) - 13/09/2016 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament rejected by 229 votes to 381, with 58 abstentions, the proposal on the nomination of Leo BRINCAT (MT) as a
Member of the Court of Auditors.
Parliament called on the Council to withdraw its nomination and submit a new one to Parliament.
