2016/0817(NLE) - 08/12/2016 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Bart STAES (Greens/EFA, BE) on the nomination of Juhan PARTS (EE) as a
Member of the Court of Auditors.
Parliaments Committee on Budgetary Control evaluated the credentials of the nominee, in particular in view of the requirements laid down in
Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union. At its meeting of 5 December 2016, the Committee on Budgetary
Control heard the Councils nominee for membership of the Court of Auditors.
Following this hearing, Members called upon the Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of Juhan Parts as a
Member of the Court of Auditors.

2016/0817(NLE) - 14/12/2016 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament delivered a favourable opinion (488 votes to 133, with 88 abstentions) on the Councils nomination of Juhan PARTS
(Estonian) as a Member of the Court of Auditors.
Parliaments Committee on Budgetary Control evaluated the credentials of the nominee, in particular in view of the requirements laid down in
Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union.
At its meeting of 5 December 2016, the Committee on Budgetary Control heard the Councils nominee for membership of the Court of Auditors
and considered that the candidate met all the necessary requirements.

2016/0817(NLE) - 19/12/2016 Final act
PURPOSE: to appoint an Estonian member to the Court of Auditors.
NON-LEGISLATIVE ACT: Council Decision (EU, Euratom) 2016/2392 appointing a member of the Court of Auditors.
CONTENT: with this Decision, the Council decided to appoint Juhan PARTS as a member of the Court of Auditors for the period from 1
January 2017 to 31 December 2022.
ENTRY INTO FORCE: the Decision shall enter into force on 19.12.2016.
