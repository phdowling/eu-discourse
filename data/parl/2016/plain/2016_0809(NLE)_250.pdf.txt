2016/0809(NLE) - 08/09/2016 Committee report tabled for plenary, 1st reading/single reading
The Committee on Budgetary Control adopted the report by Igor OLTES (Greens/EFA, SI).
The committee called on the European Parliament to deliver a favourable opinion on the Councils nomination of João Alexandre TAVARES
GONÇALVES de FIGUEIREDO (PT) as a Member of the Court of Auditors .
Following his hearing which was held on 5 September 2016, the Committee on Budgetary Control considered that the candidate met the
requirements laid down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).

2016/0809(NLE) - 13/09/2016 Text adopted by Parliament, 1st reading/single reading
The European Parliament adopted by 537 votes to 38, with 94 abstentions, a decision in which it gave a favourable opinion on the nomination
of João Alexandre TAVARES GONÇALVES de FIGUEIREDO (PT) as a Member of the Court of Auditors , having met the requirements laid
down in Article 286(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU).
